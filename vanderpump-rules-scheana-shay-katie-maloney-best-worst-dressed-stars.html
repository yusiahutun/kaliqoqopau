    The good, the bad and Kim Kardashian's BIG baby bump!

    The "Keeping Up With the Kardashians" star isn't letting her growing belly stop her from killing it on the red carpet. She wowed in white at the InStyle Awards in Los Angeles on Monday night, sporting a figure-hugging Valentino cape dress. Mrs. Kanye West completed her show-stopping look with a delicate choker necklace, smokey eye makeup and a sleek ponytail.

    Another star to turn heads at that event was Selena Gomez, who amped up the sexy appeal in a punk-inspired skirt and leather peek-a-boo top over a black bra from Louis Vuitton. While toofab's fashionistas applauded some parts of her edgy ensemble, they couldn't get over her "diaper" skirt or disheveled hair and makeup.

    We still love you, Selena!

    Iggy Azalea earned "drab" marks across the board this week after she stepped out at the Vogue China Gala dinner in a blue, feathered Armani Prive gown. The "Fancy" rapper looked frumpy in the bird-inspired dress -- and her quirky hairstyle and over-the-top makeup didn't help either.

    Check out the clip above to see who toofab's Candice Brock and "Vanderpump Rules" stars Scheana Shay and Katie Maloney break down this week's best and worst dressed stars, and take a closer look at the week's questionable fashion below.
