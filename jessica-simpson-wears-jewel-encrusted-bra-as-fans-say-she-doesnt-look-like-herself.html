    Jessica Simpson is entering her ‘icon era’ as she’s being honored with the Icon Award at the 37th annual FN Achievement Awards.

    The music star burst onto the scene in 1999. Since then has had her own hit reality TV show with ex-husband Nick Lachey, released a memoir and even her own fashion line. Being in the industry for almost two and a half decades, she’s bound to have had a number of looks. However, fans are now saying she looks ‘unrecognizable.’

    Jessica Simpson enters a new era as she’s given Icon Award

    In an interview with Foot Wear News about her achievement as an icon, the 43-year-old said: “An icon is someone who is not afraid to take risks.

    “And often, their choices don’t make sense in the immediate, but they’re part of an enlightened understanding of the big picture. It’s someone who transcends a particular time or place and defines a moment,” she concluded.

    Jessica is also entering a new music era, as she tells the publication she’ll be releasing new music in 2024.

    The new tunes are set to release early next year, with a tour to follow. When she first broke onto the scene, Simpson was only 19 years old.

    She’s known for her hits including I Wanna Love You Forever and Irresistible.

    The star looks ‘unrecognizable’

    To celebrate the award, the 43-year-old, and the publication, took to Instagram to share some pics from the shoot.

    The star can be seen posing with her iconic blonde locks in an up-do, in a range of ‘out there outfits’. From a ‘jeweled bra top’ to a floor-length shiny pink dress, she stunned in all styles.

    However, some fans failed to recognize her, and no it wasn’t because they’d mistaken her for Britney Spears.

    “She’s also entering her ‘that doesn’t look like Jessica Simpson’ era,” wrote one fan.

    Another said: “Doesn’t look like her AT ALL.”

    Others weren’t surprised she was receiving an icon award as one penned: “She entered it a long time ago! [heart emoji crown emoji]”

    As well as her ‘changing looks’ Jessica has also had comments on her weight loss, however shut down Ozempic rumors.
