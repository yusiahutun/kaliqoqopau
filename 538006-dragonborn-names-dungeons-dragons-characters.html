    What is the Dragonborn name scheme?

    A Dragonborn is named at birth, and the clan name is usually put first as a mark of honour. A nickname or childhood name is often used among clutchmates as an endearment or descriptive term. The nickname might be from an event or the Dragonborn habit.

    How do you pick a Dragonborn name?

    The first name of your fantasy character should be their clan name. The second name should describe the Dragonborn mission, appearance, or behaviour.
