    The girlfriend of late supermodel Godfrey Gao has broken her silence, two days after he died from a heart attack on the set of a reality TV show in China. 

    Taiwanese-Canadian actor and fashion star Godfrey, the first Asian model to be the face of Louis Vuitton, died aged 35 while filming TV show Chase Me in Ningbo, China on Wednesday. 

    His partner Su Xiang Han, known as Bella Su, 23, updated her Instagram biography with a poignant message which read: 'Whatever we are... You and i will always collide.'

    Forever loved: The girlfriend of late supermodel Godfrey Gao, Bella Su, has broken her silence, two days after he died from a heart attack on the set of a reality TV show in China

     Shock death: Taiwanese-Canadian actor and fashion star Godfrey, the first Asian model to be the face of Louis Vuitton, died aged 35 while filming TV show Chase Me in Ningbo, China on Wednesday

    Bella had reportedly dated Godfrey for more than three years but the couple kept their relationship largely private and seldom appeared in public together.

    The couple met through Godfrey's best friend,  former basketball player James Mao. Godfrey was due to be a groomsman at James' wedding on Friday.

     After reports emerged the star had filmed for 17 hours straight before his death, a clip from the programme shows him 'struggling to run' in the final minutes before he collapsed and went into cardiac arrest.

    This comes amid claims Godfrey reportedly filmed for 17 hours straight and cried out 'I can't keep going' before dying.

    Sina reports that Godfrey was unwell with a cold before taking part but filmed 'continuously' for 17 hours- from 8.30am on Tuesday November 26 to 1.45am, before collapsing on set.

     Much missed: This comes amid claims Godfrey reportedly filmed for 17 hours straight and cried out 'I can't keep going' before dying

    It was also claimed by his friends he attended an event on the Monday and travelled straight to filming Chase Me without taking a rest day. 

    Chase Me sees contestants work their way through a series of physically demanding obstacle courses in an urban race style setting  with the show aired on China's Zhejiang Television network. 

    According to China's Tencent News, an audience member heard Gao shout 'I can't keep going' as he slowed down in the race, before he collapsed. 

    Cameramen are said to have initially zoomed in on Godfrey, believing his fall was a dramatic moment in the reality show before realising something was wrong and getting emergency care for the star.

    Icon: Sina reports that Godfrey was unwell with a cold before taking part but filmed 'continuously' for almost 17 hours straight - from 8.30am on Tuesday November 26 to 1.45am, before collapsing on set (pictured 2017)

    MailOnline has contacted representatives for Godfrey Gao and Zheijiang Television for comment.  

    A statement from the TV network said the actor and model was taken to hospital where it was confirmed he died as a result of a cardiac arrest.  

    It said: 'On the evening of November 27, Godfrey Gao’s relatives, whom we had been waiting for, arrived at Ningbo. Our programme team are coordinating with Godfrey Gao’s management team to handle matters appropriately and in accordance with the family’s wishes.

    'All of us at Zhejiang Satellite TV have been in sorrow since the incident took place. We are gravely heartbroken by the tragedy. 

    'After his collapse, we rushed Godfrey Gao to the hospital, but we still could not save his life. For the severely consequences this ‘accident’ has caused, we feel deeply sorry.

    'Around 1.30 am on November 27, while he was in the process of recording, he suddenly slowed down while running and then collapsed, the paramedics at the scene rushed to rescue him, after which he was sent to hospital for further treatment. 

    'However, emergency treatment failed and he passed away. We will think long and hard about the reasons, investigate all the steps in the recording of the programme, and work towards implementing better security measures.

    'We would like to stress that we are there with Godfrey Gao’s family and team, and respect their wish to remain private at this time of mourning. We thank all our friends for their care and support.

    'Our love and guilt for Godfrey Gao will always be there. May he rest in peace! 

    Gone too soon:  According to China's Tencent News, an audience member heard Gao shout 'I can't keep going' as he slowed down in the race, before he collapsed

    His management company Jetstar Entertainment said in a statement posted on the Twitter-like Weibo platform that Gao had fainted and was declared dead after nearly three hours of medical care. 

    It read: 'During the early hours of November 27, while he was recording a programme, our beloved Godfrey Gao suddenly collapsed. After three hours of emergency treatment, unfortunately he left us. We are shocked and heartbroken by this loss and find it impossible to accept it.

    'We would like to thank all media friends and fans of Godfrey for extending your concerns. His agent and team were by his side throughout [the incident]. His family have rushed to the city.

    'Please forgive the sorrow and sadness Godfrey’s family are going through. We ask people to kindly leave their family alone (in this difficult time). We will help Godfrey’s family deal with the aftermaths in a low profile. Many thanks to everyone.  

    They added that everyone involved was in 'incomparable pain' and that they and the family would be organising the funeral for the star. 

    In 2011, Gao became the first Asian model to be signed by Louis Vuitton.

    Debonair: The handsome model/actor had taken to Instagram in a tux in a series of shots last week (image on the left). On the right he appears as he poses for a luxury fashion shoot in Beijing

    The model had also ventured into film, having appeared in movies in both China and Hollywood. They included the 2019 Chinese film Shanghai Fortress, and the 2013 title, The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones and The Jade Pendant.
