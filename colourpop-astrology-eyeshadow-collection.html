    ColourPop Created the Perfect Eyeshadow Palette for Your Zodiac Sign

    10 Aug 2024, 00:00

    They say eyes are the windows to the soul, so why not wear wear a smoky eye inspired by your zodiac sign? Given the world's craze with all things astrology, horoscopes, and signs, cult-favorite makeup brand ColourPop decided to release a collection of eyeshadow palettes for each sign.

    The ColourPop Astrology Quads collection includes 12 palettes (one for each sign) with a quadrant of four colors in each. And get this: the wide array of colors in the palettes were all developed and chosen based on attributes of each sign.

    The collection is also broken down into four categories or sign families (Fire, Earth, Water and Air,) so that each customer can bundle the three palettes that represent their sun, moon, and rising if they'd like.

    Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius are all fire signs, so those three palettes include warm colors like terracota, coral, and golden yellow in both mattes and metallics. The Earth group, Taurus, Virgo, and Capricorn, will feature earthy tones like green, gold, silver, and other neutrals. The Cancer, Scorpio, and Pisces palettes, or Water signs, includes cool hues of teal, lavender, and plum. Champaign, blush pinks, and sky blue colors make up the Air sign group for Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius signs.

    The dreamy collection is available now at colourpop.com for $9 each, or $27 for a family sign bundle. Scroll on for a sneak peek at the collection and find the right palettes for you based on your signs.
