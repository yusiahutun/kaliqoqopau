    Sharon Osbourne addresses Simon Cowell feud and claims she lost huge job because of him

    Sharon Osbourne has admitted she's been left &quot;fuming&quot; with Simon Cowell. The pair worked together on The X Factor for years and Mrs O has opened up about their feud in a chat with Louis Walsh during their stay in the Celebrity Big Brother house.

    Sharon Osbourne has admitted she's been left "fuming" with Simon Cowell.

    The pair worked together on The X Factor for years and Mrs O has opened up about their feud in a chat with Louis Walsh during their stay in the Celebrity Big Brother house.

    Louis and Sharon opened up about their former boss during Friday's (March 8) episode and the pair didn't hold back. Sharon went on to claim Simon ruined her chance of landing a job on The Masked Singer due to a contract obligation.
