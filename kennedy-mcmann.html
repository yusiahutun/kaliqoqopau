    An American actress as well as a singer, Kennedy McMann is known for her role in American mystery drama television series Nancy Drew. Likewise, McMann also starred in the Law & Order: SVU and in American mystery thriller film Gone alongside Chris Noth and Leven Rambin.

    Even being a new face in the industry, Kennedy has already earned a huge fan following. Not only it but she also amassed a handsome amount of money. If so, what is her net worth?

    Early Life & Parents

    Kennedy McMann was born on 30th October 1996 in Mesa, the United States under the birth sign Scorpio. As per her nationality, she is American and belongs to the white ethnic group. McMann is the daughter of Lisa McMann, an American novelist, and Matt McMann, a writer, and musician.

    Kennedy’s parents raised her along with her brother Killian. In addition, McMann completed her BFA in Acting from Carnegie Mellon University in 2018.
