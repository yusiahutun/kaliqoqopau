    You love them as much as your human family members (if not more, in some cases…). You'd never dream of feeding them anything but the finest fare, or skipping a vet appointment for a sore paw or injured claw. Your precious pets rule over your homes like kings and queens—and you wouldn't have it any other way. Why, then, is their safety on the road oftentimes an afterthought?

    Just as babies need car seats for on-the-go protection and parental peace of mind, pets deserve the highest level of security. Meet the Tavo Maeve™ + Roscoe™, a three-in-one pet car seat, stroller, and carrier travel system that merges state-of-the-art safety with high style—so you can take to the road with your furry, scaly, or feathered cargo securely in tow and look great doing it.

    Maeve™ + Roscoe™

    Maeve™ + Roscoe™

    Now 32% Off

    The first thing to know about Tavo products is that they're made by a company hailed for its sophisticated baby gear. That includes a line of pet car seats and strollers that are the first of their kind to have been put through rigorous impact testing benchmarked against United Nations Economic Commission for Europe (UNECE) child-restraint safety criteria.

    Available three sizes (determined on Tavo's website by the size of your pet), with the largest able to accommodate up to 30 pounds, the carrier—which fits into both the pet car seat base and the stroller frame—is like a first-class cabin with all the creature comforts. Not only is it outfitted with plush memory foam and peekaboo mesh windows, but it has a UPF 50+ canopy for sun protection, which secures easily with the magnetized buckle lock.

    Courtesy Tavo Pets

    The pet's car-seat base attaches to your vehicle's built-in anchor bars in the back (standard in all cars manufactured after September 1, 2002), so you can fasten the carrier in place and drive off knowing your pet is perfectly snug. There's even a stability leg that extends from the the base to the vehicle floor, which decreases rotation and reduces movement of the carrier in the event of a crash.

    And for older or tired pets that need some help, the carrier also attaches to the foldable Roscoe Stroller Frame. This unit is expertly engineered to glide over various kinds of terrain and stop with precision (thanks to its one-touch braking system), all while holding your coffee steady in its cup holder.

    It adds up to a true triple threat that's a splurge-worthy stress reducer. So while you take charge of keeping your pets happy and healthy, let the Tavo Maeve™ + Roscoe™ system handle keeping them secure.
