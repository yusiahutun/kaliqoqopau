    An escort has revealed the little-known acronyms she had to learn when she started working in the sex industry.

    Anna, from New Zealand, would tell her clients the same story of how she was a law student who decided to take up sex work so she could afford to pay her rent.

    Writing for Debate Magazine, she lifted the lid on what it's like working as an escort - and the secret codes clients used when they made a booking for her services.

    She charges at least $250 per hour, and any extras such as 'dirty talk' or the 'girlfriend experience' would be a further $50.

    An escort, from New Zealand, has revealed the little-known acronyms she had to learn when she started working in the sex industry (stock image)

    'There were so many things to learn, like acronyms (GFE, COB, CIM, DFK, meaning girlfriend experience, c** on body, c** in mouth, deep French kissing),' she said. 

    After meeting countless clients, Anna said there were many reasons why people turned to escorts - and many times they don't even want sex.

    She revealed the different people she encountered, including clients who just wanted a shoulder to cry on or a sensual massage. 

    Her job involved staying out until the early hours of the morning - and some days she didn't get any bookings so she would try to find other ways to cut down costs.
