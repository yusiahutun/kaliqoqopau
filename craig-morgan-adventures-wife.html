    14 May 2024, 00:00

    Craig Morgan is one crazy country music adventurer. He served in the U.S. Army for 10 years and was a reserve for nine. He races dirt bikes, has been in a movie and has even raced in a Swedish dog sled excursion. So, is there anything his wife Karen won't let him do?

    Morgan admits there really isn't a limit when it comes to his spouse. "My wife has never said no to anything I've wanted to try," he tells Taste of Country, admitting that he's one lucky guy.

    "I think there have been moments where she was probably thinking, 'I wish he wouldn't do that' but she would never tell me no."

    So, what kinds of adventures has Morgan asked his wife about? The 'Wake Up Loving You' singer tells all. "I was just recently considering doing a TV show 'Naked and Afraid.' I wanted to do that show. I'm a survivalist ... and I thought, 'That is the ultimate test of your survival skill set."

    'Naked and Afraid' is on the Discovery Channel and takes 'survival of the fittest' to the next level. Two strangers (male and female) find themselves stranded in and exposed to some of the world's most extreme weather environments ... naked. They have no food, water, or clothing, and must survive for 21 days.

    He continues, "I told her about it and said, 'Would you be cool with that?' because they partner you up with a woman that you've never met, and you're naked and you've gotta figure everything out."

    His wife's response? Morgan says, "If you want to do it -- I don't know why you'd want to do that. But if you want to, try it!"

    "Her big question always is, 'Are you getting paid?" And if so, how much?'" reveals the singer.

    Even though she's never said no, there is one adventure Karen gets a little nervous about. Watch the video to find out what that is.
