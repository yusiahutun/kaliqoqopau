    The national government has stepped up its efforts to digitise its services under the e-citizen platform.

    During the closure of a workshop on Friday, March 10, Immigration and Citizen Services Principal Secretary Julius Bitok said Kenyans could apply for marriage certificates through e-citizen.

    "One of the examples of services that have gone digital is marriages. If you want to get married, you can now apply for marriage certificate from the comforts of your phone where you apply online, go for interviews and get your marriage certificate online," Bitok said.

    Bitok said that a total of 5,318 services were being offered on the e-Citizen platform, calling on Kenyans to utilise it.

    He added that the government was targeting a rise in revenue from the platform, suggesting that the daily revenue may increase from KSh 80 million to KSh 1 billion.

    "The other advantage is that this will increase the revenue by reducing leakages," he said.

    Make services affordable

    Bitok further stated that using the e-citizen platform would make services cheaper for Kenyans and cut out the cost used in travelling and time spent in government offices.

    "One of the key benefits of digitisation is that it reduces the cost of services to citizens by the government," he said.

    Paybill for all gov't services

    Earlier, the National Treasury's moved to digitise government payments in January 2023.

    Treasury Cabinet Secretary Njuguna Ndung'u introduced a single Paybill number to be used across all mobile services in making payments.

    Ndung'u said Kenyans would use their National ID number as their account while foreigners use their alien ID number.

    “All payments for government services shall progressively be made through the platform,” read the notice in part," said the CS.

    Businesses and companies making payments for government services will use their registration numbers as identifiers.

    The payments will be made via the official Paybill number 222222 with a short code of 222 and the USSD code of 2222.
