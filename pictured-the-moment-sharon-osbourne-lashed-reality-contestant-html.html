    Anyone who watched The Osbournes or the X Factor knows Sharon Osbourne is not a woman to be trifled with.

    And when a contestant on her American reality show, Charm School, insulted her rocker husband Ozzy, there was no hiding place from her furious temper.

    Mrs Osbourne launched a glass of juice and then herself at former glamour model Megan Hauserman, who later filed a police complaint for alleged assault.

    Now, pictures of the incident have been released for the first time.

    Splash down: A furious Sharon Osbourne throws a glass of juice over Megan Hauserman during the Charm School reunion show

    Face off: Sharon (l) is unimpressed when Megan (r) brings Ozzy into their feud

    The explosive episode, which was filmed in December, was screened in America over the weekend and proved compulsive viewing.

    Megan, 27, had been originally 'expelled' from Sharon's Rock of Love Charm School in week four of the series.

    As she 'expelled' Megan from the charm school, Sharon said: 'You've not changed since Rock of Love (original TV show). Still the same b**chy, backstabbing girl that you are. Yeah, you changed your clothes today, but Megan, in here you're still a b**ch... a real f**king b**ch.'

    During the filming of the reunion six months later, it appeared time had done little to diffuse the bad feeling between the two women.

    Gone too far: Sharon throws a glass full of juice over Megan

    Soaked: The one-time glamour model shrieks as she comes to a sticky end


    After bikini-clad model Megan announced her chihuahua Lily was being 'fixed', Sharon advised her owner shouldn't be allowed to have children.

    Sharon, 56, said: 'I think you should join her and have that operation too.

    'I do not think you should be allowed to breed, my dear. I think that one of you is enough for any country.'

    Despite the support for Sharon amongst the audience, Megan hit back, telling her former mentor she was only famous for managing her 'brain dead rock star husband'.

    Fight: Security guards rush the stage to separate Sharon and Megan

    Megan has left the building: The model is escorted from the premises

    A fuming Sharon stood up and picked up a glass of juice and hurled it over Megan's rhinetone bikini, before jumping on her.

    While viewers are unable to see what exactly happens due to the crowd of security men pulling the two women apart, Megan alleged Sharon grabbed her and pulled her by the hair.

    Megan is quickly escorted out of the building, leaving Sharon lapping up the applause by the audience.

    She said: 'I feel so sorry for her.'

    Ready to rumble: Sharon laps up the applause after telling Megan she should be sterilised shortly before the pair fought

    Megan filed a police report accusing Sharon of pulling her hair at the reunion taping last month.

    Photos at the time showed the former Playboy model, who is is best known for her reality show appearances on Beauty and the Geek, Rock of Love and I Love Money, leaving hospital after the assault, holding her left arm in a sling.

    No-one has been charged in relation to the incident.
