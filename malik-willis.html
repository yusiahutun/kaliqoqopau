    Early Life

    Malik Willis was born on 25th May 1999 in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. The names of his parents are Harold Willis and Shastca Grier. He has a sister whose name is Destini Willis. At a young age, he showed interest in playing various sports. Malik completed his high school studies at Westlake High School and later transferred to Roswell High School. For his higher studies, he went to Auburn University and then took a transfer to Liberty University.

    Malik Willis Career

    Malik started his career at high school by playing football. After transferring to Roswell for his senior year he played and scores were 2,562 passing yards 1,033 yards rushing with 37 total touchdowns. In 2017 and 2018 he went to Auburn University. Over there he played in 15 games. He completed 11 of 14 passes for 69 yards with a touchdown and rushed for 309 yards and two touchdowns. In 2019 he was transferred to Liberty and did not play any game due to NCAA transfer rules. Next year he played in 10 games and scored 2,250 passing yards with 20 touchdowns and 944 rushing yards with 14 touchdowns. In 2021 he was seen in 13 games and scored 2,857 passing yards and 27 touchdowns and 878 rushing yards with 13 touchdowns.
