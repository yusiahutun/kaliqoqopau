    Cameron Diaz Reportedly Gave BFF Gwyneth Paltrow Wedding Advice Before She Tied The Knot

    Theyre such good friends! Before Gwyneth Paltrow married Brad Falchuk in a secret wedding ceremony on Saturday, Sept. 29, the actress reportedly turned to none other than her very good friend, Cameron Diaz, for marriage advice.

    16 Apr 2024, 00:00

    They’re such good friends! Before Gwyneth Paltrow married Brad Falchuk in a secret wedding ceremony on Saturday, Sept. 29, the actress reportedly turned to none other than her very good friend, Cameron Diaz, for marriage advice. 

    “Cameron was very excited about Gwyneth’s wedding,” a source recently told People. “Gwyneth has been giving her updates and asking for advice during the planning.” 

    Cameron, 46, seems like the perfect person to ask for advice since she’s been happily married to Good Charlotte rocker Benji Madden for three years. And with the help of her BFF, Gwyneth, also 46, was able to have the wedding of her dreams. “Cameron pulled off a private wedding even though the media found out about it before. Gwyneth wanted the same privacy,” the source continued.

    Despite the media finding out about the Iron Man actress’ secret nuptials, Gwyneth still ended up having a very beautiful ceremony in the Hamptons. Shortly after she tied the knot with Brad, the actress shared a cute photo of them both wearing their new wedding bands via Instagram. 

    According to People, Cameron was just one of many celebrity guests at the Gwyn and Brad’s romantic wedding. Jerry Seinfeld, Steven Spielberg, Rob Lowe, and Robert Downey Jr. were also in attendance. In April, Cameron also attended Gwyneth’s star-studded bridal shower and was spotted posing in a group picture with her fellow party guests on Instagram. 

    “When some of the women you love most in the world give you a luncheon and you end up with underwear on your head…,” Gwyneth captioned the funny picture at the time. In Goop Magazine’s Sex & Love January issue, the newlywed also gushed about how great Brad makes her feel everyday.

    She said, “Personally, at midlife, I have tried to accept how complex romantic love can be. I have decided to give it a go again, not only because I believe I have found the man I was meant to be with, but because I have accepted the soul-stretching, pattern-breaking opportunities that (terrifyingly) are made possible by intimacy.”
