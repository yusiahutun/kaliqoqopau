    Tiger King star Joe Exotic is now a single man again, after his divorce from third husband Dillon Passage is now finalized... just a week after teasing it was close.

    The 59-year-old Exotic (real name Joseph Allen Schreibvogel) had tied the knot with Passage, 27, in December 2017.

    Passage revealed on Instagram in March 2021 that he was seeking a divorce, and now his dissolution of marriage has been finalized in Santa Rosa, Florida, via TMZ.
