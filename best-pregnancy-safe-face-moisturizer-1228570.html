    How Does Your Skin Change During Pregnancy?

    Every female’s skin changes during the pregnancy period. These changes are classified into three main groups. First, physiological changes that are largely associated with hormones. Second, dermatoses or non-specific changes that were already existing or were triggered due to pregnancy, and lastly the specific pregnancy-related dermatoses that appear only when the woman is pregnant and get resolved in the postpartum phase (3). However, you can effortlessly take care of your skin by vouching for the best face moisturizers during pregnancy that will help you bid adieu to acne marks, dehydrated skin, rough skin, and dark spots. Well, we have also shared some tips to attain healthy skin. Scroll down and check them out. 

    How to Have Healthy Skin While You Are Pregnant?

    Here are a few tips to have healthy skin while you are pregnant -  

    1. Drink a lot of water. 

    2. Cleanse your face with warm or lukewarm water. Use a light cleanser or face wash. 

    3. Apply toner and wait until it dries off completely.  

    4. Apply a moisturizer safe for pregnancy. Massage gently. Don’t forget to apply it to your neck. 

    5. Seal your skin with sunscreen or simply use a moisturizer that is enriched with SPF. 

    6. Follow the same routine at night. 

    7. You may also apply a night cream or gel that is loaded with hydrating and nourishing ingredients like aloe vera. 

    Tada! You are done! See, taking care of your skin and paving the way for healthy skin during pregnancy is not as difficult as it looks! Now, let us run you through a detailed buying guide so that you can make some quick and wise purchasing decisions. 

    How to Choose the Best Pregnancy-safe Moisturizer?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before investing in a pregnancy-safe moisturizer - 

    Skin Type: Make sure that you analyze the texture and type of skin you possess. If you have dry, rough, and flaky skin, pick a pregnancy-safe facial moisturizer that is extremely hydrating. For those dealing with oily and acne-prone skin, invest in an oil-free and mattifying moisturizer. Do not use a cream, lotion, or moisturizer that is not meant for your skin type as it might lead to skin rashes or breakouts. If you have sensitive skin, make sure you take note of the dominant active ingredient that works towards keeping your skin hydrated and your skin barrier well-nourished. 

    Ingredients: Always pay heed to the list of active ingredients of the moisturizer you are planning to buy. An ideal face moisturizer for pregnancy should contain clean ingredients like hyaluronic acid, aloe vera, ceramides, botanical extracts, squalene, and vitamin C, A, and E that are capable enough to keep your skin hydrated. These ingredients work toward enhancing, rejuvenating, nourishing, and hydrating your skin to the maximum. 

    Formula: The best facial moisturizer for pregnancy is one that is non-comedogenic, hypoallergenic, fragrance-free, non-toxic, dermatologically-tested, and free from harmful chemicals. Make sure you stay away from creams that are formulated with parabens, sulfates, and synthetic fragrances. You may also pick a good-quality pregnancy-safe face moisturizer with SPF 15 or 30 for everyday use. It will help you protect your skin from harmful UVA and UVB rays of the sun and quench the thirst of your dehydrated skin. 

    Texture: The moisturizer should be light in weight and possess a non-greasy, and non-sticky texture. Out of our list of the best moisturizers for pregnancy, you may vouch for any that suits your skin type. All of them have a great texture and get absorbed quickly and penetrate deeply.

    FAQ's

    Is it safe to use a moisturizer during pregnancy?

    Yes. The use of a moisturizer during pregnancy keeps the skin soft and smooth.

    Which skincare routine is safe for pregnant women?

    It is advisable to cleanse the face first with a light cleanser. Then, spray a little bit of toner, and seal your skin with a pregnancy-safe moisturizer with SPF 15 or 30.

    Can you use vitamin C moisturizer when pregnant?

    Yes. You can use vitamin C moisturizer when pregnant as it is safe for sensitive skin.

    Are all face moisturizers safe to use during pregnancy?

    No. Face moisturizers containing retinoids, hydroquinone, and phthalates are not safe to use during pregnancy.

    Are organic or natural face moisturizers better for pregnant women?

    Yes. Organic or natural face moisturizers are better for pregnant women as they are safe to use, pure, and gentle on the skin.

    Can I use anti-aging face moisturizers during pregnancy?

    Most anti-aging face moisturizers contain retinoids, hence it is not advisable to use them during pregnancy.

    Can I use face moisturizers with SPF during pregnancy?

    Yes. You can use face moisturizers with SPF during pregnancy as it helps in protecting your skin from harmful UVA and UVB rays.
