    Meaning of Happily Ever After by TOMORROW X TOGETHER

    05 Jun 2024, 00:00

    The TOMORROW X TOGETHER song titled 'Happily Ever After' explores the theme of finding happiness and contentment in a world that may not always align with our expectations. It challenges the idea of happily ever after as something that is not necessarily guaranteed or achievable in real life. The song encourages listeners to embrace the unpredictability of life and to find joy in every moment, regardless of the outcome.One standout lyric from the song is "Life is not a fairy tale." This lyric challenges the notion that life should follow a linear narrative with a perfect ending. It reminds us that life is full of ups and downs, and it is important to embrace the journey rather than fixate on the destination. This theme of embracing imperfections and finding beauty in the unexpected is further emphasized in the lyric, "모르면 어때? 난 오히려 좋아" (Translation: "What if I don't know? I actually like it"). This lyric encourages listeners to embrace the unknown and embrace the beauty of spontaneity and surprise.Another important lyric from the song is "그렇게 생각했던 내가 stupid" (Translation: "I thought that way, I'm stupid"). This lyric highlights the theme of self-reflection and personal growth. It acknowledges that our initial perceptions and beliefs about life may not always be accurate or realistic. It challenges the idea that there is one "right" path or answer, and instead encourages listeners to reevaluate their perspectives and embrace the complexities of reality.The lyric "동화책을 덮어도 삶이 계속 이어져, realized 영원히 no happily ever after" (Translation: "Even if you close the fairy tale, life continues, realized forever no happily ever after") further reinforces the overarching theme of the song. It emphasizes that even when we reach the end of a chapter or experience, life keeps moving forward. There is no ultimate happily ever after because life is a continuous journey with its own unique twists and turns.The lyric "모르면 어때? 난 오히려 좋아" (Translation: "What if I don't know? I actually like it") also introduces the theme of embracing uncertainty. It encourages listeners to let go of the need for certainty and control, and instead find joy in the unknown. It suggests that surprises and unexpected outcomes can lead to new and exciting experiences.Overall, 'Happily Ever After' by TOMORROW X TOGETHER encourages listeners to embrace the unpredictable nature of life and find happiness in every moment. It challenges the idea of a perfect ending and celebrates the beauty of imperfections and unknowns. The song serves as a reminder to appreciate and cherish the journey, even when it deviates from our expectations.
