    But now people, and especially girls of her age, might feel more jealous of her as she has found a perfect boyfriend in life. She also calls her lucky to be dating her boyfriend. But who's the lucky guy she's dating? Let's find out.

    Brooke Feels Lucky To Be In A Relationship With Nolan:

    Brooke Hyland has been dating her high school sweetheart and fellow Instagram sensation Nolan Betts. The two are seen to be in total love with one another and Brooke seems to feel so lucky to have Nolan as her boyfriend and love mate.

    This tweet from Brooke shows that she feels amazed to have Nolan in her life.

    Moreover, this post on her Instagram wishing Nolan on his 19th birthday shows how lucky she feels to be dating Nolan.

But what is Brooke up to in 2017?
