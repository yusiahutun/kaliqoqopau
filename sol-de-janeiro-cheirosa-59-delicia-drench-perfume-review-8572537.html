    I’m obsessed with fragrances; my bathroom cabinet is full of luxe glass bottles that I never leave home without spritzing. Over my years as a perfume snob, I’ve learned that a good scent isn’t dependent on look, price, or brand name, so I’m proud to declare that I’ve officially found my new favorite body mist: the Sol de Janeiro Cheirosa 59 Delícia Drench Perfume.       

    I’m sure you’ve heard of the famous Brazilian brand, being that it’s racked up 2.5 billion views on TikTok, so its no surprise that its new Cheirosa 59 Delícia Drench Perfume Mist has kicked all my other fragrances to the curb. The delicious scent exudes notes of vanilla orchid, sugared violet, and sheer sandalwood, while a beautiful purple tone decorates any vanity. Starting at the affordable price of $24, the vegan and cruelty-free perfume is available in 90 and 240 milliliter-bottles, as well as a refillable, eco-friendly option. 

    Sol de Janeiro Cheirosa 59 Delícia Drench Perfume Mist

    I like to spritz the Cheirosa 59 Delícia Drench perfume mist all over my hair and body. It never feels overpowering and, instead, instantly boosts my mood, putting me in a restful state of mind. I feel confident knowing that I smell good and plan to layer the fragrance with the coordinating body butter to prolong the scent’s duration. 

    But I get it, it’s difficult to know what a perfume smells like through a screen — if only there were a scratch-and-sniff function. So, let me paint you a picture: you’re sitting on warm sand at the beach with a flower in your hair. You sip on a sweet, fruity cocktail topped with whipped cream as the sun spills down your back; you feel mature and sexy — that’s what the Cheirosa 59 Delícia Drench mist is all about.

    But don’t get it twisted; the Sol de Janeiro scent isn’t just for summer. It’s the type of fragrance you reach for after a shower, before a date, or on a trip with your girlfriends, no matter the season. It’s currently the dead of winter and I’m wearing the yummy fragrance all around. It adds warmth to any time of year, transitioning with ease thanks to its multi-seasonal notes. 

    “I can honestly say it would work all year round without feeling out of place,” said a pleased shopper. “[I’m] definitely going to make this an everyday scent,” they added. Another reviewer said they were “completely blown away” by the fragrance’s “versatile scent,” claiming it’s “suitable for any occasion,” striking the “perfect balance.”  

    The Delícia Drench mist is additionally ideal for busy, on-the-go days thanks to its lightweight, plastic bottle; just toss it into your bag and reapply as needed. If you, like me, are a frequent flyer, the TSA-friendly option is best for you.

    TL;DR, I’m obsessed with the Sol de Janeiro Cheirosa 59 Delícia Drench perfume mist and you will be, too. Snag yours and everyone will soon be ooh-ing and ahh-ing over your delectable new aroma. Plus, you can shop further scents below.
