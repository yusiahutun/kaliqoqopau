    Step aside, ballet flats—there’s a new cult footwear in town.

    It seems that every It girl lately has been relying on Miu Miu to deliver a major shoe statement—namely, via the house’s patent leather slingback pumps embellished with three crisscrossing buckle straps. Kendall Jenner has worn them, Gigi Hadid has worn them, and now, Emily Ratajkowski has officially joined the club.

    Miu Miu Kitten-Heel Slingback Pump

    Miu Miu Kitten-Heel Slingback Pump

    The My Body author sported a black, kitten-heel iteration of the viral shoes yesterday in Paris, where she spoke at Kérastase Paris’s Power Talks event at the iconic Palais de Tokyo.

    The slingbacks were a glossy accent to her classic Parisian look, which consisted of a strapless black minidress featuring a ruched bodice, a large twisted bow detail on the waist, and a bubble hem. For a pop of color, Ratajkowski added a long sheer scarlet neck scarf, tied off to one side.

    Lyvans Boolaky//Getty Images

    Lyvans Boolaky//Getty Images

    Ratajkowski recently served up another black and red ensemble while out and about in New York City this past weekend. At the time, she embraced fall fashion in a slouchy cherry-red sweater dress with a V neckline from Loewe, which coordinated perfectly with her black croc-embossed and pointed-toe knee-high boots, also from Loewe. She accessorized the look with a plain black purse from the Row and gold hoop earrings from Jennifer Fisher.

    The model described her street style in an interview with PopSugar in June 2022.

    “I love wearing sneakers with a dress, especially as it’s getting warmer in New York,” she told the outlet. “It’s the most comfortable-but-still-chic look that you can do. Other accessories, like what you do bag-wise and jewelry-wise, can dress it down. A larger bag makes it more casual, but if you do something tiny and kind of sexy, it can be great for night.”
