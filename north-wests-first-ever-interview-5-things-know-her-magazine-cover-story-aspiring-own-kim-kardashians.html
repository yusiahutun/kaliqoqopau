    In the rare interview, North talked about her hobbies, aspirations, fashion inspiration and much more. When asked to describe her life in three words, she answered: “Blessed, awesome, cool.”

    Here are five highlights from the pop culture princess’s interview.

    1. North West looks up to her dad, Kanye West

    North revealed that she’s inherited her father’s love for music and performing, saying, “I like singing. Performing is my favourite.” When asked where the interest stems from she said, “mostly from me. And then a little bit of my dad”.

    In 2020, North famously showcased her vocals for the first time at the Yeezy show during Paris Fashion Week, singing, “What are those, these are clothes.”

    According to the interview, her favourite song is Ye’s “Through the Wire” and when asked if there’s one person she wants to meet, she picked Tupac Shakur, one of the most influential rappers of all time.

    2. She’s a fashion icon at 10

    Having accompanied her mum everywhere from Paris Fashion Week to the Met Gala, North was exposed to the glitz and glamour of fashion early on. She even has her very own Michael Jackson memorabilia – his iconic velvet jacket and white fedora – that her mum gifted her for Christmas when she was six, reported Essence.

    Despite having such influential parents, North, who is quite the fashionista herself and picked out her outfit for the photo shoot from her own wardrobe, revealed that she is her own fashion icon.

    3. She’s the heiress to the Kardashian-West Empire

    North may only be 10, but as the oldest of Kim and Kanye’s four children, she has her eyes on the prize. In the interview, she revealed that, much like her parents, she wants to be a “business owner” when she grows up.

    In true nepo baby fashion, the preteen doesn’t need to look further than home to nurture her entrepreneurial spirit, announcing in the interview that one day she wants to own both Kanye’s Yeezy and Kardashian’s Skims. Ambitious, much?

    4. She has big plans for the future

    When asked what she wants to do in the future, North listed several occupations: “a rapper, a basketball player, and I’m going to make artwork that I sell. Also, one day I want to own Yeezy and Skims, and I want to be a business owner”.

    It seems she already has her future planned out, and knows exactly what she wants to do.

    5. She’s already putting her entrepreneurial skills to use

    North has a keen interest in art. Her mother has shared photos of the youngster’s impressive landscape paintings on social media, prompting many fans to speculate about the authenticity of the pieces, per US Weekly.

    This doesn’t seem to phase North though, who continues to pursue her hobby and attend art classes.

    In the interview, the young painter lamented over how expensive art equipment costs, but luckily she has a plan: “When I’m, like, 13, I want to walk dogs to make money to buy art supplies, because everything around here is so expensive.”
