    Club Bolívar has a rich history

    Established in 1925, Club Bolívar is one of the oldest and most successful football clubs in Bolivia. With a history spanning over 95 years, this iconic club has left an indelible mark on Bolivian football.

    The club has a strong fan base

    Club Bolívar boasts a passionate and dedicated fan base that supports the team through thick and thin. The Bolivaristas, as they are known, fill the stadium with their chants and cheers, creating an electric atmosphere during matches.

    Club Bolívar holds numerous domestic titles

    The club has won a record 29 Liga de Fútbol Profesional Boliviano titles, making them the most successful team in the history of Bolivian football. Their dominance in the domestic league showcases their consistent performance over the years.

    International success

    Club Bolívar has also enjoyed success on the international stage. They were crowned champions of the Copa Libertadores in 1964, becoming the first Bolivian club to win the prestigious tournament.

    Home stadium

    The club’s home stadium is Estadio Hernando Siles, located in the city of La Paz. With its high altitude of over 3,600 meters, playing at Estadio Hernando Siles poses a unique challenge for visiting teams.

    Rivalries

    Club Bolívar has intense rivalries with other top Bolivian clubs, including The Strongest and Oriente Petrolero. Matches between these teams are highly anticipated and often result in fierce competition on the pitch.

    Contributions to Bolivian national team

    Club Bolívar has been a breeding ground for talented players who have represented the Bolivian national team. The club’s commitment to nurturing local talent has helped strengthen the national squad over the years.

    Successful youth academy

    The club has a renowned youth academy that has produced talented players who have gone on to achieve great success in both domestic and international competitions. The academy focuses on developing young players and instilling the club’s values in them.

    Commitment to community

    Club Bolívar is actively involved in various community development initiatives. Through their foundation, they support social causes, provide access to sports education, and promote inclusivity and diversity within football.
