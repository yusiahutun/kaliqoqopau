    At Loro Piana, Less Is Infinitely More

    Italian fashion brand Loro Piana kicked off the celebrations for their Summer Resort 2023 collection with an intimate dinner at chef Yann Nury’s charming Shelter Island Summer pop-up.

    Friends of the label—including Will Arnett, Alessandra Brawn, Vanessa Fuchs, Sally Singer, PJ Mattan, Amanda Murray, Maryah Ananda, and Marcus Leslie Singleton—joined Loro Piana North America CEO Matthieu Garnier in the festivities.

    Looks of the night included sleek monochrome suits, dreamy linen dresses, sandals, woven totes, and airy jackets in a variety of earth tones.

    Like each attendee's refined ensemble, the Art de la Table tablescape embodied the delicious sophistication of the maison's holiday lifestyle: breezy linen placemats, handmade porcelain bowls, and fresh local flowers. After a farm-to-table dinner, cocktail hour took place in the property's lavender garden, lit up in the night by fireflies.
