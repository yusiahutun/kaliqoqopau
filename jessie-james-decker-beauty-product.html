    Jessie James Decker Just Shared Her Must Have Beauty Product

    Jessie James Decker is officially spilling her beauty secrets, and we are here for it. The mom of three looks flawless in basically every picture and video she posts on Instagram, so the second she drops any tips, tricks, or products worth trying, we're listening.

    30 Jan 2024, 00:00

    Jessie James Decker is officially spilling her beauty secrets, and we are here for it. The mom of three looks flawless in basically every picture and video she posts on Instagram, so the second she drops any tips, tricks, or products worth trying, we're listening.

    Burt's Bees 4 Pack Beeswax Lip Balm

    Burt's Bees 4 Pack Beeswax Lip Balm

    Credit: Amazon

    In an "Ask me anything" Instagram stories series, the "I Still Love You" singer shared in response to "Best all day lip product," that her go-to lip balm is none other than Burt's Bees. She captioned the pic "Can't live without it." Enough said, we're buying!

    The best part? The star apparently loves a bargain find because the lip balm comes in at just under $4 at Walmart (or $9.99 for a 4-pack on Amazon!). But just because it's an on-budget beauty find doesn't mean it's not incredibly effective. Made from a beeswax base and enriched with vitamin E and peppermint, the lip balm is both incredibly moisturizing and occlusive. Not only is it packed with ingredients that are great for your lips, but the occlusive beeswax locks all those ingredients in.

    Jessie James Decker on Instagram

    And just in case a new budget-friendly yet super-effective lip balm isn't on your must-have list right now, not to worry: the cookbook author is planning on sharing even more beauty finds. In response to a follower asking "More lifestyle/make up (sic)/skincare/hair videos???" in the same Instagram story series, the singer shared "OK I promise. I'm going to get better at this and linking and sharing my go tos!"

    Needless to say, we'll be hanging on every word and sharing them all here.
