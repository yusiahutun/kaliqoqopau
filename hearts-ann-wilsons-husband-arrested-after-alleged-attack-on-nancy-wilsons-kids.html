    Heart's Ann Wilson's Husband Arrested After Alleged Attack On Nancy Wilson's Kids

    SEATTLE (CelebrityAccess) — Authorities in Seattle have arrested the husband of Heart lead singer Ann Wilson on suspicion that he assaulted his sister-in-law Nancy Wilson's twin sons.

    According to the Seattle Times, 65-year-old Dean Stuart Wetter appeared in King County District Court on Monday on allegations that he attacked the two boys while they were backstage at a concert.

    Per the Times, Court filings state that the two boys were watching the concert from backstage and then left to walk around in the parking lot. After seeing Ann Wilson's new tour bus, they opened the door to look inside but were allegedly confronted by an angry Wetter.

    Court documents claim that Wetter started yelling at the boys and then allegedly punched one in the back of the head before grabbing him by the throat. When the other boy intervened, Wetter allegedly grabbed him by the throat as well, the Times said.

    Wetter was freed on a $10,000 bond and due in court again on August 31st, the Times said.

    Heart is currently on the road with Cheap Trick and Joan Jett & the Blackhearts in support of their new album Beautiful Broken. – Staff Writers
