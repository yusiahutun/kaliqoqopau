    Alright, hear me out—I know it’s only October, but you should order your holiday beauty advent calendars now. If you wait until November or December, your faves might be sold out and that’s not the way you want to kick off the holiday season.

    The secret about beauty advent calendars is that they’re a great way to score the most amount of items for less (when you really break it down product by product). Whether you want to try your go-to skincare brand’s entire product line or do a mix-and-match of famously good beauty products you have yet to try, you can get 12 or 24 items for a fraction of the full retail price. Plus, they’re fun. If you have a collector’s mindset or get a burst of serotonin whenever you unbox an item, you should definitely add an advent calendar to your cart. It’s basically 24 days of presents to yourself (or the loved one you gift it to).

No matter what you’re into, you can get a set that’ll fuel your obsession. And you’ll want to keep an eye on this list because we’ll be updating it periodically leading up to peak advent calendar season.
