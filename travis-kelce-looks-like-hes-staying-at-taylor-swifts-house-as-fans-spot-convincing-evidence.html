    Travis Kelce looks like he's staying at Taylor Swift's house as fans spot convincing 'evidence'

    Taylor Swift fans are not only a devoted fandom but highly skilled when it comes to being a detective, and theyve found convincing evidence that appears to show Travis Kelce is at her house. The celebrity couple are still the talk of the town seven months after they made it official at Arrowhead Stadium

    10 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Taylor Swift fans are not only a devoted fandom but highly skilled when it comes to being a detective, and they’ve found convincing ‘evidence’ that appears to show Travis Kelce is at her house.

    The celebrity couple are still the talk of the town seven months after they made it official at Arrowhead Stadium – a place close to both of their hearts in relation to their careers. Taylor Swift is enjoying a well-earned break from her jam-packed Eras Tour, while the tight end embraces downtime after winning the Super Bowl once again. But Swift fans believe they’ve found clues Travis Kelce is staying at Taylor’s house – despite buying a new, multi-million mansion in Kansas City last year. Grab some popcorn, and let’s dive in.

    Travis Kelce’s tell-tale signs he’s at Taylor Swift’s House

    In a recent mini press tour, where the 34-year-old was promoting his upcoming Kelce Jam Festival (May), he was, as expected, asked about his new beau.

    When talking to Entertainment Tonight, he gushed over Taylor’s talent, and hinted nothing will stop him from traveling to London to support the European leg of her tour this summer.

    As adorable as the conversation was, Swifties on Reddit couldn’t resist turning back the pages of time – and more importantly, Instagram.

    They worked out his wallpaper backdrop was eerily almost identical to a photo of Taylor from years ago – from her house – so is Travis Kelce moving in temporarily to the Swift residence while their schedules match up? Who knows. But Swifties love it.

    Take a look at the NFL star below:

    And now, if we dive deep into Tay’s Instagram, we find similar – if not the same – wallpaper in a selfie she took back in May 2020 during the Covid pandemic.

    Coincidence? We think not. The music star loves Easter eggs, and this is the biggest one we’ve found when it comes to Hollywood romance.

    While the celebrity did later quarantine in London with her ex-boyfriend Joe Alwyn, the pandemic stretched over two years. Therefore, this was taken at the start of the pandemic – when she was writing Folklore – and admitted how ‘lonely’ she felt. So it’s easy to deduce she wasn’t in London at this point.

    Not only that, we doubt Trav would be sitting in with Joe Alwyn in London, for the ET interview, because that’d surely be an awkward conversation between two members of her long list of ex-lovers. That’s for sure.

    Chiefs’ star gushes over the singer

    After praising her talent, and saying getting to know the Grammy-award-winning singer was an ‘amazing experience’, he complimented Taylor.

    In what we presume to be Taylor Swift’s house, Travis Kelce admitted she’s on another level within the music industry.

    When asked what he’s learned from his girlfriend, he joked: “Don’t try and be Taylor, that’s what I learned.”

    “She’s on a whole other stratosphere. She’s the best at what she does for a reason. It’s because she’s so articulate and just very dialed into every single thing that she does. And that’s the beauty of it.”

    But fans think when Trav smiles awkwardly in the interview, and looks to the side more than once, it’s because she’s sitting right there.

    Perhaps the 34-year-old was enjoying seeing him squirm over questions about her.

    It comes after an April Fool’s prank claimed the tight end was caught ‘cheating’ on the singer.

    A popular TikTok star, who compiles news round-ups each month and yearly, claimed Travis Kelce had been caught being unfaithful and it made headlines. But fans forgot to take note the clip was released on April 1st. They were duped into the fake story, and felt ridiculous for believing in it.

    Taylor and Travis’ relationship is going All Too Well. Don’t you worry.
