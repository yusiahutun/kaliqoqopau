    So, who is Noah Beck exactly?

    How did Noah Beck get famous?

    Once dubbed “the biggest TikTok guy on Earth” by GQ, Beck rose to fame by sharing content on the video platform. According to the article, his first post was a video of him lip-synching to DaBaby’s suggestive rap from Megan Thee Stallion’s “Cash S***”. The next morning, he woke up to over 20,000 followers, who were mostly “young” and “female”, he told the publication.

    Later, he shared a series of videos of himself shirtless and dancing, and he quickly grew his following to three million. Now, he has over 33 million followers and 2.4 billion likes on the app, at the time of writing.

    He works with luxury fashion brands

    As is the case with many high-profile social media stars, Beck is sought after by high fashion labels. He has been pictured at events for brands like Thom Browne, Michael Kors, Tommy Hilfiger, YSL Beauty, Balmain, Dolce & Gabbana, and many others.

    Why did Noah Beck stop playing football?

    As per The New York Times, Beck was previously an unknown university student training to be professional football player.

    British GQ added that he was Soccer Aid’s “Starboy” playing on the pitch alongside Tom Hiddleston and Usain Bolt. So why did he stop playing?

    According to his interview with the NYT, he has since put his footie dreams on hold. “I had a full-ride, division 1 scholarship doing something that I loved and that I had done my whole entire life,” he said.

    In a video shared on YouTube, he explained his reasoning, saying things changed when he joined TikTok’s Sway House. “Without thinking, I was like, ‘hell yeah’. But then I had to really think because – I’m gonna get serious for a sec – it was the hardest decision I’ve ever had to make in my life,” Noah said. “I was choosing between my dream and passion, being a professional soccer player.”

    “I don’t regret any decision I made. Everything happens for a reason,” Beck continued. “But I do miss soccer more than anything.”

    When did Noah Beck date Dixie D’Amelio?

    According to People, Beck and D’Amelio, both 22, started dating in 2020, after the latter broke up with TikTok star Griffin Johnson. However, by November 2022, they called it quits.

    In an interview on Lewis Howes’ podcast The School of Greatness, Beck said: “Everyone has their first true love, and she was mine.”
