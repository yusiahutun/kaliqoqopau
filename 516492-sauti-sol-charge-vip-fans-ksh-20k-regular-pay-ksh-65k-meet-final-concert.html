    Sauti Sol to Charge VIP Fans KSh 20k, Regular to Pay KSh 6.5k for Meet in Final Concert

    Celebrated band Sauti Sol is gearing up to bid farewell in style with an epic final concert at the Uhuru Gardens in Nairobi. The award-winning Kenyan band recently announced that they are breaking up after close to two decades of making sweet music together.

    03 Apr 2024, 00:00
    • Award-winning Kenyan band Sauti Sol is set to perform their last show together during the 2023 SolFest in November
    • The group announced they were taking an indefinite break from band activities, and SolFest will be their last performance on stage
    • The band announced the event will go down at the Uhuru Gardens in Nairobi with VIP ticket holders promised 5-star services and performance

    Celebrated band Sauti Sol is gearing up to bid farewell in style with an epic final concert at the Uhuru Gardens in Nairobi.

    Sauti Sol take break from music

    The award-winning Kenyan band recently announced that they are breaking up after close to two decades of making sweet music together.

    However, they assured supporters that it is not the end of their legacy.

    "It’s not just the end of an era, but the beginning of a legacy that will forever remain etched in the hearts of their fans," Sauti Sol wrote on Twitter.

    Tickets for the once-in-a-lifetime experience are premium, with the VIP tickets costing a whopping KSh 20,000, while the regular tickets are priced at KSh 6,500.

    VIP fans will have the opportunity to enjoy the full Sauti Sol experience, including an up-close and personal performance, a 5-star banquet complete with complimentary drinks, and limited-edition Sauti Sol merchandise.

    The tickets are limited and are expected to sell out fast.

    Boyz II Men VVIP tickets

    SolFest VIP ticket prices almost match that of the Boyz II Men concert that saw Kenyans spark a debate on the value of the tickets.

    The excitement for an up-and-close performance saw the VVIP tickets fly off the shelf in record time.

    Boyz II Men performed at the Carnivore Grounds in Nairobi on June 18th, 2023.
