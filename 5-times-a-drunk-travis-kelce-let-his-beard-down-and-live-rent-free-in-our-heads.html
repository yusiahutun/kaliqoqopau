    5 Times a drunk Travis Kelce let his beard down and live rent free in our heads

    Travis Kelce may got down in history as one of the most famous tight ends, but the drunk Trav we all know and love should receive a spot as well. One thing Travis Kelce is good at besides enjoying himself when hes a little too drunk is not caring what other people think.

    23 May 2024, 00:00

    Travis Kelce may got down in history as one of the most famous tight ends, but the drunk Trav we all know and love should receive a spot as well.

    One thing Travis Kelce is good at – besides enjoying himself when he’s a little too drunk – is not caring what other people think. Taylor Swift fans roasted the athlete over his wardrobe (remember the 1989 fit?), and his relationship has reached the next level when it comes to the strange ways people try to get a photo. In spite of all that, the 34-year-old is enjoying himself. His team, the Kansas City Chiefs, just won another Super Bowl and he bought a new mansion last year. It also seems the hottest celebrity couple on the planet are taking time to embrace their time together too. Here’s some of his best moments – never change Travis Kelce.

    5 times Travis Kelce got drunk and had a good time

    Travis Kelce drunk singing at the Super Bowl parade

    The Chiefs celebrated their victory with a rally and a parade in the city after being named Super Bowl champions once again. Before the big game, Travis himself said he wanted to get his ‘own ring’, when quizzed if he’d propose to Taylor Swift if the walked away victorious.

    He got what he wanted.

    But Travis Kelce seemed a little too drunk when he started singing ‘Friends in Low Places’ on stage. He was clearly happy with the team’s performance this year.

    We don’t think Tay-Tay needs to worry about her Grammys at this point.

    Fans loved him letting loose.

    One wrote: “Just when you think Travis can’t get any cringier… he out cringes himself!”

    A second commented: “Love that Mahomes always save the naughtiness of Travis like a protecting brother!”

    While a third hit back at fans claiming he acted like a ‘fool’. They said: “Chill out, he’s had a bit too much to drink. Can’t handle his alcohol lol. He will be feeling it in the morning and I’m sure he isn’t the only one.”

    Another Super Bowl victory

    The Chiefs have won four times so there’s likely to be a few clips of Travis Kelce celebrating, whether shouting, a little drunk or singing.

    Last year, he was seen pouring beers at Uncle Woodys bar and drinking out of the jug as he poured himself a beverage. We don’t think this was his first one!

    The 34-year-old had returned to Cincinnati to visit the bar, which is a favorite for students of the college. Although Travis Kelce took a decade to graduate college himself.

    One fan wrote: “Travis is a god of UC. Godbless him and all he’s done for our weird Midwest college.”

    Again, you can’t get positive comments without some people pushing out negativity. Some commentators were claiming he ‘wasn’t welcome back’, without giving enough reasons behind their troll answers.

    A devoted football fan came to the defense of a drunk Travis Kelce: “Everyone’s saying he’s not welcome here because he plays for the chiefs and won the super bowl? I’m so confused lmfao. I’m proud that a Cincy (and Cleveland) boy won the super bowl. 😂 He always shows his love for the city. Y’all are just mad.”

    Look what you made him do

    In November, Kelce burst into singing a song again – seems to be a staple for a drunk Travis – alongside Taylor Swift.

    The pair were enjoying a karaoke session and belted out Wheatus hit Teenage Dirtbag at his home.

    He told WSJ Magazine: “There was some wine involved, for sure.”

    One of his friends, they were ‘hitting the right notes’ in terms of their blossoming relationship. Whether Trav did the same for his singing skills, we’re not convinced.

    “[They’re] peas in a pod,” the friend said. “[It was] a sweet, magical moment.”

    Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift enjoy drunk sing-a-long to her song

    Then at the Super Bowl After Party this year, Taylor and Travis were celebrating the big win at a club in Las Vegas.

    They shared a kiss while an EDM remix of Love Story pumped through the speakers and fans loved the PDA.

    Later on, they were seen sining across the room at each other to more lyrics during You Belong To Me.

    So cute.

    They were out partying with Patrick and Brittany Mahomes, who called it a night at around 3.30am. But Trav and Taylor, both 35, kept the party going until 5.15am. They apparently ordered chicken fingers and fries too.

    Kept the good times going

    Back in 2020 when they won the Super Bowl – do you see a pattern here? – a drunk Travis Kelce out in the wild was spotted drinking beer off the Lombardi Super Bowl Cup.

    He wrote: “Growing up playing hockey, I always wanted to chug out of the Stanley Cup after being crowned a Champion…. No cup on the Lombardi, so I decided to take a ride down the Lombardi Luge!”

    One fan declared: “God Damn you are the coolest! Not to mention best TE in football!”

    While others highlighted, as this was just before the coronavirus pandemic he’s likely not going to do it again.

    If you’ve been affected by this story you can contact American Addiction Centers on (877) 686-7688. Or Talk To Frank on 0300 123 6600 in the UK.

    Jason Kelce is definitely his brother

    Now, this is a bonus because of the Kelce brothers, but Travis’ older sibling Jason seems to get up to silly antics while drunk too.

    After Travis scored a touchdown during a game against Buffalo in January, the 36-year-old ripped off his shirt and screamed towards the stands. Trav’s family and friends were watching the Chiefs game in a VIP suite at Highmark Stadium, but Jason had other plans.

    The Philadelphia Eagles center – who retired this week – joked he had about ’40 beers’ before celebrating his younger brother’s touchdown.

    “I got caught up in the energy of Bills Mafia. It is electric,” Jason told Travis later on their shared podcast New Heights.

    “I thought I gotta have my shirt off at some point and i didn’t get to do it beforehand.

    “I didn’t want have my shirt off in the suite so I thought I’m gonna take off my shirt then jump out of the box and then I can be with my Buffalo Bills compadres.

    “This is how my mind works after 40 Miller Lites.”
