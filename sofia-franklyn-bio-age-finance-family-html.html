    Sofia Franklyn is a blogger and podcaster associated with the high ranking pop culture blog titled Barstool Sports.

    She is very famous for co-hosting the podcast Call Her Daddy alongside Alexandra Cooper. She has also written various blogs for Barstool Sports on topics of sexuality and taboo issues.

    The blogger has more than 300K fans following on Instagram, and her podcast has over 10 thousand reviews on iTunes.

    Bio: Birthday, Nationality & Education

    Sofia was born on 21st July 1992 in Salt Lake City, Utah. As a native of the land of dreams, she holds American citizenship.

    The online personality has shied away from sharing most of the information about her family members, but she does posts picture with them time and again.

    After finishing high school, Sofia enrolled in the University of Utah, pursuing her education in the field of Economics.

    Eventually, she graduated and started working in a finance firm. However, the blogger had other plans in her mind as she wanted to pursue a career in a more creative field.

    Career

    The podcaster, whose height is 5 feet and 5 inches (1.6 meters), began her career in media after she moved to New York and joined Barstool Sports.

    After entering the blog in 2018, she made her debut with a fascinating article exploring her sexual adventures in her hometown, Utah. The article was a humorous introduction to her upbringing in Utah along with many references to the stereotypical view of the place and sexuality.

    Interestingly, the idea for her hit podcast arose from a wonderful evening with her friend in a bar. While working for the blog, she was sharing a room with her current co-host, Alexandra Cooper.

    One evening, during their vacation at the SXSW festival in Texas, they started to chat very frankly about s*x. Their chat attracted quite an audience in the bar, and one of them suggested their conversation would make a perfect show. This suggestion was all it took to encourage them to make their show.

    Sofia and Alexandra eventually recorded their first podcast after returning to New York, and Barstool Sports soon produced it.

    Aftermath & Net Worth

    As of now, the 26-year-old podcaster has listeners all around the globe. She and her friend Alexandra are recurring members of the podcast where they frankly talk on physical relations.

    Sofia's podcast is a blend of relationship advice, lot of kinky talks and also offers dating advice to men although many mislabel the show as centered on women only. Importantly, her empowering podcast has helped on normalizing the discussion about sexuality and taboo topics.

    Since the podcast market is growing every day, her podcast has more than 90 thousand subscribers. Due to her success in the show, she has acquired an estimated net worth of $1 million.
