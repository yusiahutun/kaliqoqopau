    Open: Tony Roma's

    Tony Roma's

    In the mood for ribs instead of turkey? While you should still check your local Tony Roma's holiday hours, most of the chain's restaurants will be open on Thanksgiving Day, serving up their signature dishes.

    Find a location.

    Open: Fogo de Chão

    Kevin Marple

    For those who prefer steak over turkey on Thanksgiving, you're in luck. The Brazilian steakhouse will be open from 11 a.m.-9 p.m. with new and traditional offerings like roasted turkey au jus, sweet potato casserole, Brazilian sausage, and apple dressing. Be sure to make a reservation in advance (kids 6 and under eat free!). If you want to dine at home, pick up the Thanksgiving Celebration To-Go Package. The meal serves six—you just need to reheat it at home.

    Find a location.

    Open: Ruby Tuesday

    Raymond Boyd / Getty Images

    Downing mozzarella sticks and devouring spinach artichoke dip sounds like an excellent way to spend Thanksgiving. At Ruby Tuesday, you can make it happen; most locations will be open on Thanksgiving Day for both dine-in and to-go orders. Be sure to call ahead and/or make reservations if you can!

    Find a location.

    Open: The Capital Grille

    The Capital Grille

    Place an order or make a reservation for a special chef-prepared dinner of Turkey with Brioche stuffing, French green beans with Marcona almonds, cranberry-pear chutney and mashed potatoes. Don't forget the Pumpkin Cheesecake!

    Find a location.

    Open: Popeyes

    Roberto Machado Noa//Getty Images

    If you've tried their legendary chicken sandwich, you're aware this New Orleans-based chain knows their way around a bird. And if you're a fan, then not only can you eat there on Thanksgiving, but you can get a whole cajun-flavored turkey as well! It's pre-cooked, but you'll need to heat it up again in your own oven. Just give your nearest location a call ahead of time (or order online to have one shipped) to place the order—but don't wait. They do sell out.

    Find a location.

    Open: Buffalo Wild Wings

    Rick Diamond / Getty Images

    Many—but not all—Buffalo Wild Wings locations around the country will be open on Thanksgiving Day. You'll want to check with the closest one near you to confirm their particular hours. You'll be able to tune in to the big game while you're there!

    Open: Metro Diner

    Metro Diner

    Metro Diner has you covered whether you want to dine in or take it to go. Choose from specials like the Stuffing Waffle with Turkey, the Sausage & Gravy Chicken Tenders Waffle, or a more traditional holiday dinner. Locations are open until 2:30 on Thanksgiving Day.

    Open: Ponderosa and Bonanza Steakhouses

    Ponderosa and Bonanza Steakhouses

    Ponderosa and Bonanza Steakhouses have locations throughout the U.S., and their buffets will be open with roasted turkey, baked ham, fried chicken, meatloaf, shrimp, mac-and-cheese, stuffing, mashed potatoes, sweet potato casserole, green bean casserole, cranberry sauce, fresh baked rolls, and dessert. They also offer to-go platters starting at $12. Check the restaurant locator to find hours and locations.
