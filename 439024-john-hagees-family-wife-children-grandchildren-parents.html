    John Hagee's biography

    Who is John Hagee? He is an American pastor and televangelist. He was born on 12th April 1940 in Baytown, Texas, USA. The pastor was raised in a conservative household by Reverend and Mrs Bythell Hagee.

    John joined Trinity University to study History and Education before joining the University of North Texas for his master's degree. Later, he joined Southwestern Assemblies of God University for a Diploma in Theology.

    Career

    On Mother's Day of 11th May 1975, John founded a church at Castle Hills. Although it began with 25 members, he built a new sanctuary with a 1,600 seating capacity within two years. Over the years, the church continued to grow.

    On 4th October 1987, he dedicated a 5,000 seat sanctuary and named it Cornerstone Church. John and his second wife, Diana, received their anointing from Dr W.A Criswell, pastor of the First Baptist Church of Dallas.
