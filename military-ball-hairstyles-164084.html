    Military ball hairstyles may seem a bit intimidating at first, especially if you’ve never attended an event like this before. But once you pick which look you want, you’ll be surprised by how rewarding the end result can be. However, this begs the question: Where should you start when you’re trying to find the best military ball updos? Even if you’ve lost track of how many military balls you’ve attended, it can  sometimes be difficult to find cute military hairstyles that stand out among the rest. Luckily, there are some beautiful formal hairstyles for a military ball available for you to see on Instagram — and we rounded up some of our favorite ones to inspire you.

    The Best Military Ball Hairstyles

    (Photo Credit: Instagram/hairby_lauraashley)

    To first understand what makes great military ball hairstyles, you must first understand what the military ball you’re attending is all about. According to Military.com, military balls are formal events held for a wide variety of reasons, including homecomings, birthdays, and other forms of celebrations. Of course, a military ball is always meant to honor the military service, which is important to keep in mind when selecting your beautiful hairstyle — and picking out the rest of your outfit! 

    That said, it’s worth noting that no two military balls are exactly the same. As Sarah Peachey, a military wife writing for Military One Click, notes, some involve seated dinners and others have buffets. Some have guest speakers, while others do not. Peachey recommends asking a service member or military spouse who has attended a function from the same unit about what to expect. Naturally, many women look forward to these types of events as an excuse to dress up and go all out with their whole beauty routine — including their fabulous military ball hairstyles.

    But if you think you’re only allowed to wear updo hairstyles for military balls, you are totally mistaken. As military wives dish on Spouse Buzz, a forum on Military.com, it’s completely fine to wear your hair down if you want. Sometimes opting for easy military ball hairstyles might be a better option for you, especially if you have an extremely elaborate dress. From short military ball hairstyles to military ball hairstyles for long hair and everything in between, there’s a great military ball hairstyle out there for just about everyone.

    It’s worth mentioning that you don’t necessarily need to be attending a military ball to don incredible military ball hairstyles. Some folks chose to wear military ball updos for their wedding days, while others decide to try military formal hairstyles for other fancy events. You should feel very free to explore and experiment until you find a military hairstyle that is perfect for you.
