    Design expert Nate Berkus is known for hosting The Nate Berkus Show (2010-2012). He was also the resident interior designer at Oprah Winfrey Show from 2004 to 2011. 

    Nate is also an author known for The Things That Matter (2012) and Home Rules: Transform the Place You Live Into a Place You'll Love (2005).

    Coming Out 

    Nate came out as gay during his college years. He knew about his sexuality from age 8 or 9 but chose to keep it a secret. However, Nate’s stepfather found out about it via his love letter. Nevertheless, he supported Nate. 

    Heartwarming Coming Out Story:- Openly Gay Dan Howell Coming Out Story & Parents Details

    The design expert has opened about his sexuality in his semi-autobiographical book, The Things That Matter. 

    Nate Berkus' Married Life With Children 

    Nate has been married to Jeremiah Brent since 3rd May 2014. The duo has two children: a daughter named Poppy (born in March of 2015) and a son named Oskar (born in March 2018).

    Nate Berkus( Middle) with husband Jeremiah Brent and children in November 2019 (Photo: Nate Berkus' Instagram)

    Poppy and Oskar were born via surrogacy. 

    Who Is Jeremiah Brent?

    Jeremiah is a furniture and interior designer. He is the founder of Jeremiah Brent Design, a successful design firm. He also worked on the TV show, Nate & Jeremiah by Design (2018-2019) with his husband, Nate. 

    Wedding Details

    Nate and Jeremiah were friends for a long time. They happened to meet at a mutual friend’s birthday party, which led to an antique shopping date. The date turned into a life long courtship for the couple. 

    Celeb Wedding:- Nicole Williams Engaged To Wedding Details, How Much Is Her Worth?

    Nate and Jeremiah got engaged in 2013 in Peru. They got married a year later at the New York Public Library as the first same-sex couple to get married at the esteemed location. The wedding was officiated by Sheri Salata. 

    Death Of Nate’s Ex-Partner 

    Nate was previously dating Fernando Bongoechea. The pair was on vacation in December 2004 in Srilanka when Tsunami hit the country. Sadly, Fernando passed away. 

    To honor his ex-partner, Nate named his son Oskar, which was Fernando’s middle name. 

    Net Worth 

    Nate’s net worth is $18 million, according to celebritynetworth.com. 

    Facts On Nate Berkus Including Age

    • Nate was born on 17th September 1971 in Orange County, California, USA. He is a Virgo. 

    • His birth name is Nathan Jay Berkus.

    • Nate stands at the height of 5 feet 9 inches (1.75 meters). 

    • He has a design firm named Nate Berkus Associates. 

    • Nate has a younger brother named Jesse Berkus. 

    • His famous clients include Oprah Winfrey, Jerry O'Connell, and Kirstie Alley. 

    • Nate shares a home with his husband and children in Los Angeles. 

    • The reality star put his Greenwich Village Home on sale for $800K in 2019. 

    • Nate has won House Beautiful's Next Wave Design Talent (2003), and Craine Business Magazine's 40 Under 40 To Watch (2003) 

    • He was named Chicago Social Magazine Design Director in 2000.
