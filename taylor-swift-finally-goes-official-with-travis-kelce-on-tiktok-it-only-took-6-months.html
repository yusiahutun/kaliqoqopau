    Taylor Swift has finally made it official with Travis Kelce after sharing a funny video on TikTok. The clip shows the singer and tight end partying the night away after the Super Bowl on Sunday.

    It only took six months, but we’re not mad about it. The celebrity appears to have finally made her relationship with Travis Kelce official on social media with a new video on Taylor Swift’s TikTok. The celebrity couple may pack on the PDA when out on the field, but they keep their social media accounts professional. Neither of them have given an outpouring of love on Instagram, and they don’t even follow each other. They’re probably too cool for that. But now, we’ve finally received what we’ve all been waiting for, authentic footage of Taylor Swift and Trav living their best lives on TikTok.

    Taylor Swift and Travis accidentally go clubbing with her parents on TikTok

    The funny video shows Travis grinning ear-to-ear as the 34-year-old is dancing in the club, in what appears to be a VIP section.

    Then Taylor, also 34, then pans over to look at the crowd in the club, which is absolutely packed. She moves the camera around to show her parents, Scott and Andrea, who are sitting down and may not be enjoying it as much as they hoped to.

    The Lover hitmaker then shows her own reaction, which is a bit awkward, as she didn’t plan on going clubbing with her parents.

    She wrote in the TikTok caption: “Accidentally going clubbing with your parents is something everyone should try at least once in their life.”

    She also added on top of the clip: “It’s a friends and family party they said”.

    Jason Kelce wins the Super Bowl after party

    It comes after Twitter users had fun sharing clips of Trav’s big brother, Jason Kelce, having the time of his life after the Super Bowl.

    The center, whose team Philadelphia Eagles faced the Kansas City Chiefs last year, donned a wrestling mask and was hanging out with the DJ in the decks. He’s never shy from letting loose, because it was only last month when he ripped off his shirt and screamed at the top of his lungs when Travis scored a touchdown. The image of his celebration sparked a huge meme reaction, and the athlete is now selling t-shirts with that moment and raising money for charity.

    The duo have a close bond at only being two years apart and share their own Football podcast, New Heights. They talk about the latest news in the sport as well as fan reactions, Swifties and memes.

    However, a few weeks ago he revealed his wife Kylie Kelce was on the lookout for a new pet and he wasn’t keen. Kylie wants a cat, which Jason isn’t on board with, even if it meant she could bond with Taylor Swift over the new furry friend.
