    Sharon Osbourne looks stylish in all-black ensemble while shopping in LA

    Sharon Osbourne and her husband, rock legend Ozzy Osbourne, celebrated their milestone 40th wedding anniversary on the 4th of July. Two days later, and the talk show personality decided to head out to do some shopping with a friend along the world famous Melrose Place in West Hollywood.

    29 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Sharon Osbourne and her husband, rock legend Ozzy Osbourne, celebrated their milestone 40th wedding anniversary on the 4th of July.

    Two days later, and the talk show personality decided to head out to do some shopping with a friend along the world famous Melrose Place in West Hollywood.

    The mother of three looked to be in great spirits as she flashed a big beaming smile moments after buying an item or two at SPRWMN clothing store, which she followed up with a stop to pick up a hot cup of coffee.

    Retail therapy: Sharon Osbourne, 69, looked to be in great spirits when she headed out for a day of shopping along the world famous Melrose Place with a friend on Wednesday

    Osbourne, 69, showed off her fashion sense by stepping out in an all-black ensemble, consisting of ultra-baggy pants with a matching top and black and white boots.

    The London, England native appeared to laughing out loud when the cameras caught her making her way from the cafe, all while trying not to spill her coffee.

    With the sun glaring down and the temperature hovering close to 80 degrees Fahrenheit, the former host of CBS' The Talk made sure to wear large-frame sunglasses for their walk along the a stretch of shoppes and restaurants.

    Stylin': The talk show personality showed off her fashion sense in ultra-baggy pants with more fitted top and funky black and white boots

    Ozzy, 73, had hoped he and his wife would renew their vows and celebrate their 40 years of marriage on the Hawaiian island of Maui, but it appears as though his operation on Monday thwarted those plans.

    The Black Sabbath frontman had to have surgery to adjust pins inserted into his neck and spine following a near-fatal ATV crash in 2003 and subsequent fall in 2013.

    Instead of soaking up the sun in Hawaii, the couple spent their special day at the Beverly Hills Hotel, where the couple got in plenty of rest and relaxation.

    Afterwards, Sharon took to her Instagram page to give her 1.1 million fans and followers a glimpse at some of the party favors they enjoyed, and to thank the hotel and its staff for all their hard work.

    Milestone: Sharon shared some of the spoils she and husband Ozzy Osbourne enjoyed when they spent their 40th wedding anniversary at the Beverly Hills Hotel

    Tasty time in Beverly Hills: There was plenty of sweets and snacks, along with photos of the couple taken over their years

    Festive: The Osbourne's hotel room was full of balloons and other party favors

    'Thank you @bevhillshotel & all the staff for spoiling us on our anniversary! Your hotel holds a special place in our hearts,' she wrote in the caption.

    The post included a look at a photo collage pasted on a mirror in their hotel room, along with two wrapped presents, an assortment of tasty sweets and treats, a mini two-tie cake, as well as bundle of balloons and fresh red roses.

    Two days earlier, that actual day of their anniversary, the couple both posted sweet sentiments to their partner along with a throwback photo.

    '2022 is a special year for me., Sharon began in the caption, adding, 'It marks 40 years of marriage to my darling Ozzy. We first met when I was 18, over 52 years we have been friends, lovers, husband & wife, grandparents and soulmates. Always at each other's side. I love you Ozzy ~ Sharrrrrrron x @shelbyduncan.'

    The so-called Prince Of Darkness posted of picture of the couple on their wedding day with the caption: '40 Years Ago Today! Happy Anniversary My Love.'

    Sweet sentiments: The couple enjoyed a mini two-tier cake as part of their anniversary celebration at the Beverly Hills Hotel

    Lovely: The two-persona party was sealed with  batch of fresh red roses

    Sharon had been across the pond in the UK but flew back to Los Angeles to be by her husband's side for his surgery.

    Three days after the operation Ozzy thanked fans for their support and confirmed that he was 'recuperating comfortably' at home after undergoing 'major' spinal surgery.

    'I am definitely feeling the love and support from all my fans and send everyone a big thank you for their thoughts, prayers, and well wishes during my recovery,' he shared on Twitter. 

    Ozzy is expected to finish out his recovery from the comforts of his Los Angeles home for the foreseeable future.

    The legendary rocker, who's also been diagnosed with Parkinson's disease, previously suffered a nasty fall and dislodged metal rods placed in his spine following a quad-bike accident in 2003. 

    The couple previously renewed their vows in Las Vegas in 2017.
