    Naked and Afraid XL is one of the popular American reality TV shows. The show is a new generation of extreme survival skills and features some famous survivalists. So, what do you know about Naked and Afraid XL contestants?

    Who are season 9's Naked and Afraid XL contestants? Season nine features some of the popular names you have probably heard of. These contestants undertake some of the most brutal environments for 40 or 60 days and nights. Here is everything you need to know about the contestants.

    Naked and Afraid XL contestants

    The ninth season premiered on July 31st, 2022. It is set in the frozen Rocky Mountains of Montana, United States, and is named Naked and Afraid XL: Frozen. According to BuddyTV, the Survivor XL cast season 9 is made up of the following.

    1. Jake Nodar

    Jake is a naturalist, adventurer, actor, activist, environmentalist, and horse trainer. He came into the limelight because of his appearance on Naked and Afraid XL. His previous challenges include seasons two and six.

    Jake was born in Baltimore, Maryland. He was raised alongside an older brother, one younger brother, and two younger sisters. His love for horses started at a pretty young age. He even attended a horse training school in western Colorado.

    2. Gabrielle Balassone

    Gabrielle Balassone is a television personality who gained prominence for participating in Naked and Afraid XL. She is an enthusiastic and diligent lady with diverse experience. She completed law school at the University of Baltimore and sat for the Maryland State Bar Exam.

    Gabrielle completed the fourteen-day fan challenge in the African wilderness in 2017. She returned again in season nine.

    3. Waz Addy

    Waz Addy is one of the contestants of season 9's Naked and Afraid XL. He is a well-known television personality and a survivalist. He hails from Sydney, Australia. He was raised alongside three sisters, Peta Addy, Sara Judd, and Chelsea.

    Waz has already proven himself in past seasons of Naked and Afraid. He lasted an impressive 95 days in the wild. He showcased his ability to adapt to harsh conditions and outwit the challenges nature throws his way.

    4. Ky Furneaux

    Kylie "Ky" Furneaux is an Australian outdoor guide, television host, survival expert and stuntperson. She has appeared in over 100 films and TV productions. She was born on October 20th, 1973, in Adelaide, Australia.

    Ky's parents, Peter and Loy Burford, are both retired teachers. She is the younger of two children. Her older sister Shauna is a business consultant.

    5. Sarah Bartell

    Sarah Bartell is known for Landon Cruise: Reiki Master and New Mexico Rising! She is new to XL but was a contestant in seasons 10 and 11 of Naked and Afraid. She is a survivor, taxidermist, homesteader and self-confessed mother of wolves.

    6. Trish Bulinsky

    Trish has become famous because of her involvement in the reality television show. She was a housewife before joining the show. Unfortunately, not much about her has been shared publicly.

    Trish Bulinsky last appeared on XL season 8. She was also a contestant during the tenth season of Naked and Afraid.

    7. Jeremy McCaa

    Jeremy McCaa is no stranger to the wild. He grew up hunting, fishing, and mud-bogging in Monroe. He is a Louisiana native and is a seasoned survivalist with multiple stints on the popular show.

    8. Wes Harper

    Wes Harper is a survivor in the truest sense of the word. His passion for the outdoors includes watching various hunting, fishing, and survival programs on TV. He is also well-known for Deadpool: Just Another Day.

    9. Jermaine Jackson

    Jermaine Jackson is a sales marketing executive, survivalist, and reality TV personality. He was born on March 5th, 1974. He previously worked for a surgical solution at 3M before becoming an acute trainer at 3M Medical Solutions.

    10. Sara Burkett

    Sara Burkett is an adventurer, survivalist, and reality television personality from Maryland, Tennessee. She is best known for her appearance on the television series, Naked and Afraid. She is married to her husband, Bryan Burkett, and they share two sons, Bentley and Austin.

    11. Joe Ortlip

    Joe has arisen as one of the most notable TV big names because of his ability to survive. He has amassed a sizable following via online entertainment. Because of his work as a survivalist and TV character, he has procured colossal riches.

    12. Rod Biggs

    Biggs is a survivalist and reality television personality from Wichita, Kansas. He is a family-orientated, passionate educator. He is married to Erikja Biggs, and they are parents to four children, two daughters and two sons.
