    Greta Lee’s red carpet looks have been the saving grace of the 2024 awards season. Her SAG Awards look—an off-the-shoulder gown from The Row, paired with leather evening gloves—was a masterpiece. Her bright red gown at the Academy Museum of Motion Pictures Gala was elegance epitomized. And Greta Lee’s 2024 Oscars look? Well it’s to die for, obviously. Greta Lee’s stylist Danielle Goldberg styled her in a gorgeous black and white gown from Loewe.

    While many expected Lee to earn a “Best Actress” nomination for her performance in Past Lives, she didn’t receive one. Past Lives earned two Oscar nominations, including one for “Best Picture.” And if the film wins the award, you can expect Lee to take the stage alongside her cast mates.

    Lee’s groundbreaking style may be new to some. But fans know she’s been delivering cutting-edge red carpet looks for nearly 10 years. Lee showed up to the 2019 Emmys in a neon green two-piece that felt bold, playful, and elegant all at once. Her sleek black gown at the 2023 CFDA Fashion Awards was a celebration of statement necklines. And her poofy black dress at last year’s WIF Honors was a sheer delight.

    Lee’s 2024 Oscars red carpet look will undoubtedly stand out in a sea of classic silhouettes and conventional choices, but it’s far from the first time she’s given us a red carpet moment worth celebrating.
