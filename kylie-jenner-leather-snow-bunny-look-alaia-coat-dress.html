    Kylie Jenner dipped into the archives for an enviable winter street-style look.

    The Kylie Cosmetics founder and The Kardashians star was photographed while out and about in Aspen, where celebs including her sister Kendall Jenner and Hailey Bieber have spent the last week of the year. For the outing, the star wore an edgy cold-weather look, styling a retro flared coat by Alaïa as a minidress.

    She layered the leather and shearling piece, which was originally designed in 1987 and features an asymmetrical zipper and oversized collar and cuffs, over a beige turtleneck, sheer tights, and knee-high black leather boots with white stitching. Her accessories included black shades and gloves, a furry black bucket hat, and a structural pyramid bag.

    The beauty mogul also shared the look on Instagram, posing in front of walls of framed belts.

    Kylie Jenner//Instagram

    The reality star celebrated Christmas Eve alongside her family last week, as she and her four-year-old daughter Stormi Webster attended the luxe Kardashian-Jenner family holiday party. For the fête, the mother-daughter duo wore matching gowns by Mugler adorned with black lace. Jenner chose a sexy, 1920s-inspired silhouette for the one-shoulder nude-toned gown, completing the look with nude tights that completed the visible black stitching motif in the dress, as well as strappy lace-trimmed heels.

    Meanwhile, the adorable toddler wore a one-shouldered ballgown, made with black lace at the torso and a voluminous nude-gold skirt. Stromi's hair was pulled into a ballerina bun on the top of her head, while her mom's hair wore a Betty Boop-style bob.
