    Who is Gordon Lightfoot?

    Gordon Lightfoot (November 17, 1938 – May 1, 2023) was a Canadian guitarist and singer-songwriter. He is regarded as the greatest Canadian songwriter and his songs were recorded by some of the world’s most famous musical artists. Several of Gordon’s albums achieved gold and multi-platinum status internationally.

    His successful albums that established him as a renowned singer-songwriter include Summer Side of Life, Don Quixote, Sundown, Old Dan's Record, Cold on the Shoulder and more.

    In the early 70s, Gordon Lightfoot was romantically involved with Cathy Smith. Their beautiful relationship inspired his songs Rainy Day People and Sundown. Sadly, Cathy Smith died before they formalised their marriage.
