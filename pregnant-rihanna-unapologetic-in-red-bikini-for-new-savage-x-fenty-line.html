    Pregnant Rihanna was “unapologetic” in her latest social media post as she bared her growing baby bump in a stunning red bikini for a new Savage x Fenty photoshoot. The mother of one posted a string of images on Instagram to announce the launch of the collection.

    The 35-year-old music star knows how to make a bold entrepreneurial and fashion statement on social media. Rihanna promoted a new line from her lingerie brand Savage x Fenty and fans had many compliments for her glowing look as she expects her second child with rapper A$AP Rocky.

    Pregnant Rihanna stuns in red bikini for Savage x Fenty line

    Pregnant Rihanna glammed up for the launch of a new Savage x Fenty collection. She bared her growing baby bump in a red bra and matching underwear with long straight locks and a golden bracelet on her left hand.

    The mother of one posted the stunning string of snaps on Instagram on Monday (July 10), followed by the caption: “New fav’s ….sheer x group dropping [right now]! Head over to savageX.com.”

    Rihanna’s unapologetic personality always shines through in all of her work and her bold look attracted an array of opinions and comments from her fans.

    One person wrote: “You have got to have the best pregnancy photos so far.” Another added: “Aint nobody does it like Riri.”

    A third one chimed in: “When I tell you she can’t do no wrong…RiRi is a whole World Series, World Cup, Wimbledon, and keeps knocking it out of the park.”

    Rihanna is pregnant with her second child

    The Diamonds singer confirmed her second pregnancy during her iconic Super Bowl halftime performance back in February.

    Rihanna and A$AP Rocky announced that they were expecting their first child together in January 2022. They made the surprise pregnancy announcement with an adorable photoshoot taken in the streets of New York City, exposing Riri’s baby bump to the cameras.

    The musician gave birth to a son four months later, on May 13, 2022, named RZA Athelston Mayers. The family of four-to-be landed the British Vogue March 2023 cover where Riri shared the first official pictures of her firstborn child.

    Rihanna and A$AP Rocky worked together before falling in love

    Rihanna and A$AP are one of the hottest couples in the music and Hollywood industry right now, but they crossed paths professionally on several occasions before falling in love.

    They joined forces for Rihanna’s 2011 single Cockiness (Love It) from her sixth studio album Talk That Talk and later performed the song together at the MTV Video Music Awards.

    Rihanna and A$AP formed a close friendship and Riri invited the rapper on her Diamonds world tour. A$AP returned the favor and invited Riri to appear in the 2013 music video of his single Fashion Killa.

    A$AP was the first one to spill out the beans on his rumored romance with the We Found Love songstress when he told GQ that Rihanna was “the love of his life”.
