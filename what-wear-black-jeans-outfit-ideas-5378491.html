    Sorry to our sweats, but if we have to pick a favorite piece in our wardrobe, it would probably be a classic pair of black jeans. The perfect, slightly stretchy option can be worn for hours on end, and there's no shortage of ways to style them, whether you're looking to get sexy with a cutout top, or need to throw on winter boots before trekking through the snow.

    These babies are so versatile that they're spotted on street style stars and celebrities alike — which is good news for us since we're always in search of fun fashion inspiration. Whether you're in a pinch and want an easy, no-frills sort of look, or are stumped on how to make these bottoms feel fancy, check out the black jeans outfits ahead, which will surely help solve any sort of style dilemma.

    Go With a Western Vibe

    Reach for a shirt with some playful piping, then slip into some brown knee-high boots.

    Keep It Easy With All Black

    Then add a splash of color with your bag.

    Show a Little Skin

    If you really want to make a statement with that blue denim layer, style it over a bralette.

    Try a T-Shirt and a Trench

    It's true — this jacket option makes every outfit seem sleek.

    Slip on a Black Sweater

    Rooney Mara created an easy yet enviable weekend outfit, slipping on checkered Vans for an extra punch.

    Or a Neon One

    The layer will really stand out against an all-black ensemble.

    Play Around With Length

    If you want to make black denim cutoffs work for the cooler months, style them with longer boots.

    Remember Accessories

    A beret or even a wide-brim hat will add some interest to a black-and-white pairing.

    Think Super Sweet

    Replace grunge items with pops of pink, bow details, and babydoll shoes.

    Throw on a Blazer

    An oversized choice will make your look more laid-back, rather than formal.

    Stick With Simple Items

    Skip the stress and reach for some staples, like Nina Dobrev's black tank top and black boots.

    Add a Scarf

    That tiny detail will instantly make your outfit more interesting, no matter what you have on.

    Break Out the Old Reliables

    Black jeans styled with a leather jacket and black boots creates a timeless combination.

    Wear a Graphic Tee

    Don't overthink the shoe choice — black Converse will do!

    Show Off Your Cardigan

    Whether you go with a bright color or a matching, coordinated set, this trendy piece is a way to give basic bottoms a more modern twist.

    Bring on the Bomber Jacket

    Do as Lucy Hale did and layer it over a simple T-shirt, then complete the look with casual sneakers.

    Make a Statement With Color

    Kendall Jenner had all eyes on her when she paired these versatile pants with a red sweater.

    Layer Up

    Not only does a turtleneck under a button-down look cool, but it's also very warm as well.

    Let Your Shoes Steal the Spotlight

    White, lug-sole boots will really stand out against darker winter colors.

    Stay Comfy

    When all else fails, throw on your favorite hoodie for an off-duty vibe.
