    Coats are mandatory in the winter, but they don’t have to be boring and basic! Skirted coats are this season’s must-have piece. Skirted coats are distinguished by extra fabric in the coat that flows smoothly over the hips, and can appear with peplums, pleats, and bias cuts. Skirted coats combine the ultra-flattering cut of circle skirts and the warmth and practicality of winter coats. The cut of these chic wool coats will accent your feminine figure and add curves on a straighter body.

    Pretty Peplum
    This bright wool peacoat pairs a waist-nipped silhouette and a flouncy peplum. Topstitching, metallic buttons, zipper accents, and faux leather piping make this coat cute, but not too sweet! The rich red color will also help you stand out in a sea of black winter coats.

    GUESS Skirted Wool Blend Coat in Rouge, $118.90, nordstrom.com

    Artistic Asymmetry
    This wool coat is perfect to wear to a winter soiree or to add excitement to an outfit! The asymmetrical collar and wooden buttons of this coat are perfectly balanced in the beautiful gathered tiers, which hide useful side pockets.

    Tier for the Party Coat in Spice, $499.99, modcloth.com

    Plaid
    This stand-out skirted coat is made of a luxurious plaid, which because of the contrasting black sleeves and color, won’t overwhelm your lovely outfit. This coat fits snugly through the torso and then flares out over the hips. The rippling hemline is unique and adds some rocker edge to the plaid!

    Laundry By Shelli Segal Plaid Skirted Coat, $116.97, nordstromrack.com

    Retro Vibe
    This cute coat is a great way to incorporate the retro trend into your wardrobe without wearing someone’s old, worn-out clothes. Its understated design and neutral color make it a great transitional piece into the spring. When the weather gets warmer, wear this coat open to show off a fabulous scarf!

    Intrigue All About It Coat in Camel, $169.99, modcloth.com
