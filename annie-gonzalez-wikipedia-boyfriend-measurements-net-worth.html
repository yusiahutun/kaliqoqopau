    Gentefied, an American comedy-drama, officially returned for its 2nd season in 2021. The first season of the show had premiered on Netflix on February 21, 2020, whereas the 2nd season arrived on November 10, 2021.

    For starters, Gentefied is the story of three Mexican-American cousins, namely Erik Morales, Ana Morales, Chris Morales. The show also chronicles their struggle to chase the “American dream,” even while that the dream could potentially shatter their dearest things: their immigrant grandfather, their neighborhood, the family taco shop.”

    In this article, we will be talking about the actress Annie Gonzalez, who plays the role of  Lidia Solis, in Gentefied. We will talk about Annie’s role on Gentefied, her boyfriend, net worth, family, and more.

    Annie Gonzalez On Gentefied

    As we all know already, Annie portrays Lidia Solis, Erik Morales’ highly educated girlfriend.

    Gentefied explores the diversity of Los Angeles’ Latinx community through the eyes of Erik, Ana, and Chris. Since she is the girlfriend of one of the main characters, her character too is equally important in the whole storyline.

    When Annie’s character Lidia was introduced in Season 1, she was just a college graduate who had moved back home to Los Angeles with the hopes of creating a difference in her community. As time goes by, Lidia started dating Erik and soon they become girlfriend-boyfriend. Lidia also ended up pregnant with Erik’s child. In the 1st season itself, the two welcomed their daughter, Delfina.

    Towards the end of Season 1 of Gentefied, Erik’s granddad Casimiro “Pop’ Morales, or the patriarch of the Morales family, was arrested. With Pop taken into custody by the ICE detention center, Erik and Lidia had to deal with the burdens of parenthood alone.

    Although Pop is released from the ICE in Season 2 of Gentefied, their troubles do not get any less. Erik and Lidia move to Stanford for Lidia’s new job as a professor. It is while they are there, Erik and Lidia’s tension starts to rise. Though Lidia hires a new assistant, Diane, to help her with her job, she ends up stressing Lidia, even more, when Lidia finds out that Diane was blowing off her wok to smoke with Erik.

    Finally, in the Thanksgiving episode of Gentefied, their tension explodes. Although the two have a huge fight, they happen to get a happy ending. At the end of Season 2, Erik proposes to Lidia, which she gleefully accepts.
