    Owen Warner has already left I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here! fans swooning after he stripped off to use the jungle shower in recent scenes.

    The Hollyoaks actor, 23, sent viewers wild during Monday's (November 7) episode of the ITV show as he showed off his ripped six pack.

    However, he looks completely different in throwback snaps from his younger days as a teenager.
