    The Countdown star wore the flattering outfit on Lorraine

      host Rachel Riley has worn the most flattering dress for an appearance on . The TV presenter was on Thursday morning's show to give blood with Dr Hilary Jones for . Rachel wore a fitted sky blue dress for the show, which looked amazing on her curvaceous figure. The dress had short sleeves, a high neck and fell above her knees.

      There are very few people who can carry off such a skin-tight dress but it was no problem for slender Rachel, who often wears figure-hugging outfits on . We particularly love the stripy ribbed fabric of the dress that gives the block colour a modern twist. Rachel teamed the outfit with some summery flat sandals.

      Rachel posted a full-length snap of her dress on Twitter as she told fans of her appearance on . The star wrote: "Mission accomplished!!! That was easy, good deed to start the day ticked off!" One follower wrote: "Good on you! Important work & so fashionably done!"
