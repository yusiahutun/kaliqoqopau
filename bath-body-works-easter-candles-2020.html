Well, as long as you can press “add to cart” in time. Bath & Body Works Easter candles for 2020 dropped and the entire Easter collection was just about sold out. We’re not sure if it’s because we’re all home and have bored trigger fingers, or we’re really just that excited for spring goodies. Or maybe this year’s collection is just that good.
