    Angelina Jolie has refreshed her hair just in time for spring.

    On Wednesday evening, the star walked out of her Atelier Jolie flagship store in New York City with noticeably lighter locks. Her signature dark brown tresses had been replaced with wispy blonde shoulder-length layers, styled as a loose blowout with a slight honeyed undertone.

    While Jolie is possibly one of the most famous brunettes in Hollywood, she has slowly been taking the blonder route for quite some time now, as evidenced by a few street style sightings in early January and late December. Since then, she has clearly decided to take the fair-headed plunge.

    This also isn’t the first time we’ve seen Jolie go full blonde. She previously dyed her hair for her Oscar-winning performance in 1999’s Girl, Interrupted and, more recently, wore a platinum-blonde wig for 2021 Marvel movie Eternals.

    BACKGRID

    The new hairdo stood out in stark contrast to the rest of Jolie’s ensemble, which consisted of the typical neutral palette she’s been following all winter long. This included a black double-breasted trench coat worn buttoned atop a plain white T-shirt, as well as a pair of black-rimmed sunglasses.

    To accessorize, she added a black courier bag designed by Giancarlo Cipri, which featured white all-caps lettering across the shoulder strap of a quote from Girl, Interrupted: “Some I’ve seen, some never again. But there isn’t a day my heart doesn’t find them.”
