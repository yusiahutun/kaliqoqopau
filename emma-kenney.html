    Career and Professional Life

    Talking about her career, She has appeared in several short and made-for-television movies. Kenney was the youngest filmmaker to be a finalist at the New Jersey International Film Festival at Rutgers University in 2009 when she was just nine years old.

    In the comedic drama Shameless, Kenney played Debbie Gallagher, William H. Macy’s on-screen daughter. At the age of 10, Kenney, a student at Park Middle School in Scotch Plains, New Jersey, won the part.

    Kenney was scheduled to appear in Roseanne’s 10th season, which served as a revival of the show and debuted on ABC on March 27, 2018, according to a September 2017 announcement. She plays Harris Conner Healy, the titular character’s granddaughter.

    Following that, ABC ordered The Conners, a Roseanne spin-off that would not feature Roseanne Barr but would keep the rest of the main cast, including Kenney. The Conners made their debut on October 16, 2018.
