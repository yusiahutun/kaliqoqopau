    CAMERON MENZIES is aiming to take the World Darts Championship by storm but could face his partner.

    The darts star is dating fellow thrower Fallon Sherrock, who is also set to fight for progression at the tournament.

    Sherrock, 29, faces Jermaine Wattimena as she hopes to get to the second round at the Alexandra Palace tonight.

    Menzies, 33, is also looking to progress to the next round as he is set to face Rusty-Jake Rodriguez.

    They will both be supporting each other at Ally Pally as they are together.

    The couple met each other while playing in an online darts league and competing in a doubles team.
