    Garissa - A bus belonging to Umma Express Ltd has been swept away by floods at Tula Bridge near Lager Areli in Garissa county.

    Umma bus accident

    Tana River County Commander Ali Ndiema confirmed the incident, saying 51 passengers were onboard when the bus was swept away.

    According to the police report, the bus with registration number KDN 059X was travelling to Nairobi from Wajir.

    "A motor vehicle registration number KDN 059X made Mitsubishi bus of Ummah Bus co-driven by one Hassan Turbo was from Wajir heading towards Nairobi via Garissa and upon reaching within the Chuma Mrefu area while attempting to cross a flooded section of the road following a heavy downpour, the bus slipped out of the road and got stuck into a ditch. As a result, the bus was slowly swept by the water and got stuck around 30 metres off the highway," the police report reads.

    Why KeNHA closed the Nairobi-Garissa Road

    The incident follows a notice from the Kenya National Highways Authority (KeNHA) informing the public of the closure of a section of the Nairobi-Garissa Road.
