    Disney+’s Ahsoka, a part of the Star Wars franchise and a spin-off from the series The Mandalorian, is scheduled to be released sometime in 2023. It has been long confirmed that Rosaria will be playing Ahsoka, reprising her role from The Mandalorian Season 2.

    In an article published on November 29, 2021, Deadline confirmed that actress Ivanna Sakhno (popular for films like The Spy Who Dumped Me and Pacific Rim: Uprising) joined the show and will play a newly created character. Below is a wiki article on her.

    Ivanna Sakhno Relationship Status: Is She Dating Anyone Or Single?

    Rarely does Ivanna Sakhno post about her personal life on the Internet, which is why it is so difficult to say whether she is single or dating anyone.

    In 2020, Ivanna Sakhno had a great time with her on-screen boyfriend Alex Hafner during the filming of the movie Let It Snow. Ivanna played the role of Mia in the film.

    Also, ever since Ivanna Sakhno and SNL legend Kate McKinnon worked together in the 2018 movie, The Spy Who Dumped Me, there’s been rumors all around that the two date each other.

    On August 20, 2018, an Instagram account named @katemckinnonupdate posted a picture of Kate and Ivanne together in Central Park. On May 9, 2019, a Twitter user again claimed that Kate was seeing Ivanna.

    However, none of these rumors have ever been confirmed by Kate or Ivanna. Neither Kate nor Ivanna ever bothered to address the questions about their personal life to the public.

    That being said, the High Fidelity star revealed that it was fun and she learned a lot of things working with Kate McKinnon in the movie, The Spy Who Dumped Me, in an interview with Flickering Myth.

    She said, “…I find Kate to be incredibly talented so watching her do it has been really a teaching moment for me. I got to, I think, find love for comedy on a whole new level by working with her and I couldn’t appreciate her more.”

    Ivanna Sakhno Movies And TV Shows

    Ivanna always wanted to work in Hollywood. With Kyiv Post, she recalled drawing the Hollywood sign and putting it on the wall of her childhood bedroom and dreaming that one day she will make it there.

    Ivanna had her first acting role at the age of 6, thanks to her filmmaker parents. It was in the sitcom called Lesia + Roma.

    For the first time, Ivanna fell in love with cinema at 8 when her mother showed her the popular French movie, Amelie. Blown away by the film, she rewatched it a number of times. She also found herself attracted to the film industry outside of Ukraine.

    Soon, she started dreaming of moving into an English-speaking country, learning English, and following her dreams. At the age of 13, she left Ukraine to study English at a high school in Vancouver, Canada.

    It was there when Ivanna was discovered by Janet Hirshenson and Jane Jenkins at a casting workshop. In 2018, she came to the attention of Katelyn Feuling, who invited Ivanna to move to Los Angeles and encourage her to pursue her acting dreams.

    Ivanna moved to Los Angeles with her mother at the age of 15. She studied at Beverly Hills High School and later at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.

    Her first Hollywood role came in the 2016 thriller film The Body Tree. Ivanna made a breakthrough with the 2018 films, Pacific Rim: Uprising and The Spy Who Dumped Me.

    In 2020, she played the role of Kat Monroe in two episodes of the show High Fidelity.

    Some of Ivanna’s other Acting credits include Ivan the Powerful, Let It Snow, Can’t Take It Back, Frodya, and Chornyi Tsvetok, among others.

    What Do We Know About Ivanna Sakhno Family?

    As written earlier, Ivanna Sakhno was born into a filmmaking family. Her mother, Galina Kuvivchak-Sakhno, is a filmmaker, and her father, Anatoliy Sakhno, is a cinematographer. They introduced Ivanna to the real cinema at a young age.

    Ivanna’s mother Galina’s popular works are Scream, Zdravstvuy, mama!, Zhenskie slyozy whereas her father’s popular works are A Harp for My Beloved, Zhenskie slyozy, and The Narrow Bridge.

    Ivanna also has a brother named Taras Sakhno, who turned 29 years old in 2022, in her family. Taras is an actor by profession and some of his works are Dedushka moey mechty (2005), Dobroe utro (1995), and Sakvoyazh so svetlym budushchim (2006). He is a musician as well and has already put out an album called New Life.
