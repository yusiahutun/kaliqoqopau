    American TV meteorologist Maria LaRosa, who works for The Weather Channel (TWC), has hosted several shows since joining the channel in 2010, including First Outlook, Your Weather Today, Morning Rush and Wake Up with Al.

    She currently works as a field reporter and on-camera meteorologist and is co-host of Weekend Recharge with Paul Goodloe. LaRosa was one of the ten crewmembers assigned by the TWC to cover the disaster of Hurricane Harvey in 2017 when she was stationed in San Antonio.

    Maria LaRosa Bio

    Maria LaRosa was born on 7 November 1976 in Mahopac, New York. However, she grew up in Ramsey, New Jersey. She describes herself as a girl from New Jersey, although she was not born in Jersey.

    As an adolescent, LaRosa was a reserved science freak who preferred to bury herself in her science and math books rather than talk to friends. Young LaRosa was probably too absorbed in her books to even notice the weather and to talk less about it, to discuss it with other people.

    She attended Pennsylvania State University. When she enrolled, she had no idea what career path she would pursue, but a single visit to the university’s meteorology department was enough to convince her. Subsequently, LaRosa graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Meteorology in 1998. Before graduating, she worked as a teaching assistant for the introductory course in meteorology at the university.

    After graduation, LaRosa began her television career as a meteorologist and presenter for WTVM-TV, the ABC station in Columbus, Georgia, from 1998 to 2000. According to the New Jersey weather woman, the news director of WTVM-ABC took a risk with her and gave her a weekend commercial. She was also a news reporter on the same channel.

    After about a year and a half, LaRosa got a spot on the morning show, first as a news anchor and later as a weather presenter. In late 2000, she joined WXIX-TV, a Fox member company in Cincinnati, Ohio, where she worked as a weekend weather presenter for four years. In 2004, LaRosa moved to KYW-TV, a CBS channel in Philadelphia, where she worked as a meteorologist on weekday mornings and lunches.

    In 2010, she moved to The Weather Channel, where she began working as a co-host with Todd Santos on the weather program Weekend Now and PM Edition. Between 2012 and 2014 she hosted Morning Rush and Wake Up With Al.

    She also co-hosted America’s Morning Headquarters’ flagship morning show with Sam Champion and Mike Bettes in 2015. She is currently co-hosting another weekend show Weekend Recharge with Paul Goodloe.

    LaRosa is a member of the National Weather Association and has received the Association’s seal of approval for broadcasting, which is the new CBM (Certified Broadcast Meteorologist).

    This weather woman, like all of us, also has her favorite season. According to her, she enjoys everything that autumn brings, the weather (predictable), food, and holidays. She seems to be an indoor person as she likes to spend time with her family, cooking, and watching movies.

    Husband and Family

    Even though LaRosa is almost always on television, very little of her private life can be seen in the media. However, we know that she is married to Mariusz Kolakowski. Kolakowski comes from a very wealthy family and is the only child of his family. He also holds a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Pennsylvania State University. He currently runs a business near their home in Atlanta.

    The couple has three sons. They welcomed their first son Michael in 2005 and the second, Justin, was born in 2008. They welcomed the youngest Tyler on May 3, 2010. The couple currently resides in Atlanta with their children.
