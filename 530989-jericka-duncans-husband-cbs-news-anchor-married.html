    Who is Jericka Duncan?

    She is an American media personality. She was born on 12th August 1983 in Aurora, Ohio, United States, to Ronnie Duncan and Vyonne Duncan. Her father is a veteran broadcaster and sports director at the CBS 19 in Cleveland.

    Also, her father owns a Slam Dunk Production company, which produced an award-winning documentary based on Cleveland's historic championship team in 1964. Does Jericka have any siblings? Yes, she has a brother, Joshua Duncan and a sister, Jasmine Duncan.

    After her primary school, she joined Aurora High School in Ohio, where she graduated in 2001. In high school, she played basketball and was part of the school's track and field team. She set five records for Aurora High School as a track and field player.

    Later, she enrolled at Ohio University, Athens, Ohio, to pursue a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication. She continued running track and field at the university, where she was the school team's captain. In 2005, she received the NAACP Image Award for Athletics. She graduated in 2005.
