    Kate Middleton's Coziest Fall Look Yet Will Inspire You to Wear a Sweater Vest, Too

    Princess Kate fully embraced this seasons cozy autumnal vibes with her latest outfit. This morning, the Princess of Wales ventured on a solo outing to the Vsi Razom Community Hub in Bracknell, where she learned more about the organizations work supporting Ukrainian refugees.

    29 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Princess Kate fully embraced this season’s cozy autumnal vibes with her latest outfit.

    This morning, the Princess of Wales ventured on a solo outing to the Vsi Razom Community Hub in Bracknell, where she learned more about the organization’s work supporting Ukrainian refugees.

    For the occasion, Kate eschewed a suit, which she has typically worn during her public engagements since returning to royal duties full time back in early September. Instead, she relied on cozy knit layering and slouchy silhouettes for an easygoing all-gray ensemble.

    The princess wore a ribbed knit sleeveless turtleneck in a stone gray color from Cefinn, which she layered over a white long-sleeved collared blouse. For her bottoms, Kate opted for pleated speckled gray trousers with a high-rise and straight-leg fit by Sezane. She continued the monochrome theme with a pair of gray tweed pumps from J.Crew, which featured a multitone plaid print and a black tassel adornment on the pointed toe.

    Other accessories include pearl-studded gold hoop earrings by Shyla and a silver Cartier watch, both of which she wore to a joint engagement with Prince William the previous day.

    Kate’s royal style has undergone a subtle transformation since she resumed work following the end of the Wales children’s summer vacation last month. While the princess’s wardrobe is known for its bounty of midi and coat dresses, she has lately taken a liking to suits.

    She has recently appeared in an array of monochromatic suits, like the forest-green Burberry set she wore last week, but has also mixed and match various jackets and pants, such as when she wore a bright orange blazer with slim black trousers during a visit to a Kent County medical center.
