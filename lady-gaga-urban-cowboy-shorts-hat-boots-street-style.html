    Leave it to Lady Gaga to outdo herself.

    The "Perfect Illusion" singer has been spotted out and about over the past few weeks in a series of eye-catching outfits that include very tiny hot pants, and her latest look was no exception. For an outing in Los Angeles, the superstar donned an oversize black T-shirt tucked into a pair of the shortest denim shorts we've seen. She topped off the pieces with a quilted silver bomber jacket and a Western-style leather belt. The 30-year-old beauty gave her ensemble a cowboy twist by adding a bedazzled white hat and studded black booties. Metallic aviator sunnies completed her look.

    Tiny bottoms have been Gaga's garment of choice as of late. Mother Monster rocked a similar pair in blue in her new music video, was recently snapped in the Big Apply wearing a silver version, and donned a black style to take in close friend and designer Brandon Maxwell's New York Fashion Week show.
