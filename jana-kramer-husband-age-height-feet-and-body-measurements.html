    Bio

    Jana Kramer, born on December 2, 1983, is a country musician and actress who has seamlessly and successfully combined both careers. Jana was born in Rochester Hills, Michigan. Her parents were Nora and Martin Kramer. Together with her brother Steve, she attended Rochester Adams High School.

    After taking a break from the line-up of One Tree Hill to invest more time in her music, Jana received a record deal with Elektra Records in February 2011. She immediately set about recording her promo track “I Won’t Give Up”. The title was used as the soundtrack in the One Tree Hill episode “Holding Out for a Hero” and reached number 75 on the US Billboard Hot 100 Charts.
