    Shakira's style streak is far from over.

    The singer stepped out in England over the weekend in an unexpected street look composed of a denim corset top and leather pants.

    The top was strapless and featured trim embellishments in geometric shapes and stripes across the bodice. The pants featured pockets, strings, and flaps throughout. She finished the look with black leather heeled boots, a matching mini handbag, and black oversize shield sunglasses. (See her full look here.)

    Nicolas Gerardin

    Shakira went full glam for the outing—at the British Grand Prix—opting for bronzy makeup and a voluminous curly hairdo.

    The "TGQ" singer has been showing off her fashion all over the world as of late. Last week, she was in Paris for Fashion Week, and famously showed up to the Viktor & Rolf fall/winter 2023–24 Haute Couture show in an eye-catching look.

    The star of her outfit was an icy-white coat dress with a giant NO sign protruding from the bust, which hailed from Viktor & Rolf’s Fall 2008 collection (full of NO designs). The seams of the wrap dress were embellished with golden metal and white crystals—even on the belt, sleeves, and collar. Shakira paired the maximalist piece with strappy golden platform sandals and a matching clutch bag by Aquazzura. She additionally accessorized with big rose-tinted sunglasses by Carrera.

    Also in Paris, she attended Fendi's Haute Couture fall/winter 2023-24 show wearing a white ribbed tank (with seemingly two necklines) and black wide-leg pants with a pleated skirt curtain attached to the front. She accessorized with glossy platform heels and large black sunglasses and gold rings with the Fendi logo.
