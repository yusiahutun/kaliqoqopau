    Caesar from Black Ink Crew biography

    The reality TV star was born on 5th June 1979. He is 41 years old as of 2021. He was raised in The Bronx, New York City in the United States. He has not offered any information about his parents.

    He studied at the High School of Graphic Communications and Arts in Manhattan. Later on, he joined Katharine Gibbs College for a Degree in Graphic Arts. He graduated in 1999.

    Career

    The New York City native began his career at the age of 32 by opening a tattoo shop. His main motivation was to provide a better future for his daughter. He chronicled the day-to-day activities and dramas of the shop. As a result, the tattoo parlour in Harlem gained the attention of VH1 producers, who offered to feature the parlour.

    The Black Ink Show premiered in January 2013, which marked the beginning of Ceaser's career in reality TV. He introduced a spin-off Black Ink Crew: Chicago, which premiered on 26th October 2015. This resulted in the remarkable growth and popularity of the show. At the moment, it is running in its seventh season.
