    How to Choose the Best Armani Colognes?

    Here are a few factors to consider before getting your hands on the best Armani cologne - 

    • Scent: Giorgio Armani offers a wide range of colognes, each with a unique scent profile. It’s essential to understand your needs and preferences in terms of what fragrances you like and what occasions you wish to wear them for. Buy colognes that are long-lasting and versatile so that you never have to think twice before spritzing them on yourself wherever you head. 
    • Price: Armani colognes come with high sillage and captivating notes, making them a must-have in your collection. While they can be expensive, the quality and fragrance cast a spell so strong that you hardly regret the money spent. Addedly, there are some options available under $50, ensuring people with different budgets can buy Armani colognes. Having said that, it’s also important to understand that a higher price doesn’t always mean better quality or a better scent. Don’t be afraid to try out different options to find your perfect match. 
    • Season: Different colognes work better in different seasons — spring, summer, or winter. While lighter, refreshing scents are perfect for summer and spring, warm, spicy, and earthy scents are better suited for winter months. 
    • Size: Consider your budget and needs before picking an appropriate size of Armani cologne. They are available in different sizes to cater to your everyday and special needs so choose wisely.

    Editor’s Tip: How to Apply Armani Colognes?

    Here are a few easy and simple tricks to make your Armani cologne last long - 

    Step 1: Cleanse and moisturize your skin well. 

    Step 2: Apply Vaseline on your pulse points and warm areas on the body.

    Step 3: Spritz your favorite Armani cologne on the above-mentioned areas.

    Step 4: Layer up with scents of the same family, if needed!

    Tada! You are done!
