    Neels Visser is a model and YouTuber who is always in the public eye—so it's not surprising that he has a devoted nightly skincare routine. In this episode of Go To Bed With Me, Visser walks BAZAAR.com through his favorite products and tips for sensitive skin.

    Shop Neels's Routine

    Though it's a common skincare myth that your pores can open and close based on water temperature (they can't)—Visser likes to splash his face with warm water before shaving at night. "It hurts a lot less for me if I shave in the evening and then go to sleep with my face recovering and resting," he says. "Versus if I do it the next morning, my face gets itchy and red."

    Visser's skin is sensitive, so he reaches for conditioner instead of shaving cream. He isn't picky about which one, though. "I use conditioner from the hotel I am in, I use conditioner from my house, I use conditioner from anywhere," he says.

    After shaving and rinsing with hot water, Visser moves on to washing his face. "I use this iS Clinical Cleansing Complex. This is something someone recommended to me about two months ago, and I started using it every day since. It makes my face feel really fresh," he says. He applies a generous amount to his hands, works it into a lather, then works the cream all over his face—paying extra attention to areas like his nose where blackheads are common.

    Because his skin is acne-prone, Visser chases his cleanser with the iS Clinical Active Serum. The formula contains naturally-derives sources of glycolic, lactic, and salicylic acid to brighten and retexturize skin. "It kind of feels like peppermint on your face," he adds. His best acne-fighting advice? "Don't eat any candy, drink a lot of water, and don't touch it," he says. "One thing I like to do to prevent it from growing in,if I see something coming in, someone once said that if you put toothpaste on your pimples it will dry them out." He likes to apply a small dot of Sensodyne toothpaste on any forthcoming zits.

    Visser then massages his under-eyes using the Jack Black Eye Balm before moving on to moisturizer. His favorite is the Kiehl's Facial Fuel Moisturizer. "This one is great because it's nice and fresh feeling, it smells really nice and it energizes your face when you put it on," he says. The model warms up the formula in his palms then slathers it across his face, ears, and neck.

    The last step before he hits the pillow? Cologne, naturally. Visser loves Valentino's cologne and buys it over and over again.
