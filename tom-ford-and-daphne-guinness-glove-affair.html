    Tom Ford and Daphne Guinness' glove-affair

    This has to be our favourite picture of the week. Taken at a private party hosted by Jay Jopling last night to celebrate Daphne Guinness collaboration with jewellery designer Shaun Leane, Tom Ford plants a kiss on a slumbering Daphne  and we love it.

    The mid-party installation marks the culmination of Guinness and Leanes creative pairing, fittingly called Condra Mundum  which means Against the World in Latin. Daphne showcased an 18-carat hand crafted, diamond encrusted, chain-mail armour glove whilst lying, swathed in a lace veil, suspended on a Perspex box.

    Guests at the party included Puccis Peter Dundas, Sarah Burton and Lily Cole fresh from her graduation. Perhaps the stylish crowd could shed some light on the whether Fords lip-locking was enough to awake the Sleeping Beauty...

    Isla Cunningham
