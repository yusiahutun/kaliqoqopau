    Sheryl Swoopes married, Eric Jackson on June 7, 1995. Her ex-husband Eric Jackson was her high school sweetheart and the couple lived together for some years. They have a son, Jordan Eric Jackon who was born in 1997.

    Sadly, the couple planned to end their relationship and divorced in 1999. After Jackson, and Swoopes engaged with Alisa Scott, together the couple has a son, even though Sheryl's second marriage ended up, in 2011. Currently, she is in a relationship with Chris Unclesho, to whom she got engaged in 2012.

    Swoopes announced herself as a lesbian in October 2005, she said “it doesn’t change who I am. I can’t help who I fall in love with, no one can discover that I'm a lesbian, just sort of happened much after in life”.

    Swoopes started her WNBA career during the 1997 inaugural season when she was recruited for the Houston Comets. During her playtime with Houston, she got 2,000 career points, 500 career rebounds, 300 career assists, and 200 career steals. She was a four-time WNBA Champion from 1917 to 2000 and won WNBA Defensive Player, three times in 2000, 2002, and 2003.
