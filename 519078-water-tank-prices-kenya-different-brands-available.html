    Water supply in Kenya is characterized by low levels of access, especially in rural areas and urban slums. If you live in an area that experiences intermittent water supply, you may have no option but to purchase a water tank to store large amounts of water. But how much do you know about water tank prices in Kenya?

    TABLE OF CONTENTS

    Water tanks come in different shapes, sizes, and materials. However, the most popular water tanks are manufactured from plastic because it is affordable and durable. In terms of shape, the cylindrical vertical tanks are the most popular. The size of cylindrical water tanks ranges from 50 litres to 30,000 litres.

    Water tank prices in Kenya

    Water tank prices vary depending on various factors, such as the material, brand, and location of the seller. Here are all the interesting details you need to know about water tank brands available in Kenya and their respective prices.
