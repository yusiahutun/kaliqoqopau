    You hear about serums and toners transforming the appearance of many peoples’ skin, but what about moisturizer? We’d argue that this product deserves to be a staple in your skincare regimen, but we’d also place this atop your list of items that can help alter skin for the better. Our favorite? The Super Hydrator from U Beauty.

    This face moisturizer is absolutely next-level, keeping skin hydrated for up to 48 hours. It differs from its counterparts because it transports five unique sizes of hyaluronic acid molecules into the skin. This sustains the skin’s moisture levels by providing intense, multi-level hydration.

    With regular use of this product, your complexion should appear bouncier, more nourished, and very, very plump. What also makes U Beauty’s Super Hydrator special is that it creates a protective 3D shield to lock in moisture all day long. Use it alone or as a primer underneath makeup, providing a radiant base for any type of look.

    Many reviewers agree, saying, “Absolutely love this product! This is a repeat buy, I’ve been using it for around six months. I get so many compliments about my skin and as I’m almost 50, I’m thrilled about that!” The same user claims that one pump is enough to cover their entire face, ensuring that the product lasts a long, long time. They also swear the formula is so gentle and doesn’t cause any irritation.

    It can do all this thanks to the long-chain hyaluronic acid and antioxidants in the ultra-hydrating moisturizer. This MVP of an ingredient ensures moisture can reach more deeply into the skin than ever before. The product’s other ingredients, purified oat extract, shea butter, avocado oil, grapeseed oil, squalane, and organic argan oil, work in unison to strengthen the skin’s barrier and smooth and nourish the complexion.

    Shoppers notice quite a fast difference once they incorporate it into their skincare routine, with one stating that “within two weeks, my skin was smoother and more radiant. My face looks younger and I no longer feel the need to use makeup on a daily basis.”
