    Big Brother star Kinga Karolczak looks worlds apart from her 2005 TV stint.

    The reality TV contestant shocked the nation 18 years ago after she took a wine bottle for an X-rated spin.

    At the time, Kinga hit the headlines after she got up close and personal with the beverage bottle as she sat on the floor of the BB garden and pleasured herself with it.

    Kinga's outlandish moment went down as one of the most shocking moments on UK TV. In 2012, Kinga spoke of her wild ways and said: "On Big Brother they want you to go crazy so give you loads of alcohol. And drinking makes me behave like a completely different person. So, I just put the bottle between my legs, ran into the garden and did it again. I know I didn't even lift up my nightie. I didn't have sex in the pool or in the house, but everyone's labelling me as the slapper."

    Kinga also became known in the house for kicking up a fuss with her co-stars and was often involved in clashes. She finished fourth place on the programme and continued to try to stay in the public eye after that.

    Since her BB stint, she's also starred on Big Brother’s Little Brother, Big Brother’s Big Mouth, The Charlotte Church Show and The Sunday Night Project. Following this, Kinga also starred in a low-budget film called Cash and Curry alongside Steps star Lee Latchford-Evans. In October 2008, she also cameoed as herself in Charlie Brooker's E4 horror series Dead Set, where zombies entered the Big Brother house.

    In more recent years, she's ditched the blonde bombshell look and opted for brunette locks instead. According to reports, Kinga has also lost over three stone and put her focus into her YouTube channel titled The Kinga Show. On her channel, Kinga updated fans about her weight loss transformation, makeup, and fashion purchases.
