    07 Feb 2024, 00:00

    We may be in the midst of winter, but Megan Thee Stallion still found a way to wear a naked dress to a party.

    In fact, the Grammy-winning rapper has been wearing multiple heart-racing naked dresses to various events over the past few months, with the trend becoming something of a signature look for her.

    She wore her latest naked dress this past weekend for Saturday Night Live’s after-party, following her surprise appearance on the show (hosted by Jacob Elordi) during Reneé Rapp’s debut musical performance. En route to the fete, Rapp and model Cara Delevingne held hands with the musician, who looked unperturbed by New York City’s icy temperatures in a totally sheer, floor-length dress made of brown mesh fabric. Ruching details at the side created a clingy effect, and Megan paired the piece with matching underwear and strappy platform heels in a pale beige. To accessorize, she added a glittering diamond necklace.

    Rapp—who collaborated with Megan on the track “Not My Fault” from the new Mean Girls movie musical—and Delevingne coordinated in all-black looks. Rapp wore a cropped jacket over a white T-shirt, plus high-waisted black jeans and black boots. Meanwhile, the model wore a black leather trench coat over a black top, black skinny jeans, and black combat boots.

    BACKGRID

    Megan has frequently dabbled in the see-through trend for major red carpet events. For instance, at the Mean Girls premiere earlier this month, she walked the carpet in a translucent purple trumpet gown with a plunging geometric neckline, designed by Natalia Barzilai. And at GQ’s 2023 Men of the Year party last November, Megan opted for a bedazzled sheer gown with romantic black lace trim from Salih Balta’s Spring/Summer 2024 collection.
