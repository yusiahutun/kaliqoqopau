    Chrissy Teigen Wore Pants Covered in Feathers to Beyonc's Concert

    Fans, royals, and Hollywood celebrities alike brought out their best shimmering silver party looks to see Beyonc in concert this summer, but Chrissy Teigens outfit may take the cake for the most fun Renaissance World Tour look yet.

    11 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Fans, royals, and Hollywood celebrities alike brought out their best shimmering silver party looks to see Beyoncé in concert this summer, but Chrissy Teigen’s outfit may take the cake for the most fun Renaissance World Tour look yet.

    The model and cookbook author shared photos of her fit on Instagram earlier this week, and we simply can’t get over her extravagant Atelier Biser pants, which were completely covered in white feathers. Teigen wasn’t afraid to mix textures and sparkle, as she paired the statement pants with a Balmain tube top covered in silver glitter and white faux pearls. She finished the look with silver heeled sandals, giant silver hoop earrings, and glittery silver eye shadow. Her hair was slicked back into a curly low ponytail, and she wore a red mani-pedi.

    Ever the doting—and stylish—husband, John Legend matched Teigen in a silver Dior monogram bomber jacket layered over a plain white T-shirt and paired with white jeans and white sneakers. He also wore a silver watch and his gold wedding band.

    “Once in a lifetime artist!!!! (@beyonce, not john),” Teigen joked in her Instagram post caption.

    “Rude. Also true,” the singer responded in the comments section.

    Teigen and Legend also went to see Blackpink in concert in August. “Finally saw @blackpinkofficial and phew I GET IT. That is A SHOW. what’s the best fan page for me to follow, I need to learn everything there is to know in very little time,” Teigen wrote in an Instagram post at the time.

    For the night out, she wore a black three-piece set consisting of a bra, leggings, and a long blazer entirely covered in silver rhinestones. She finished the look with pointy silver heel with little bows in the front. Legend matched her in a black T-shirt and leather jacket.
