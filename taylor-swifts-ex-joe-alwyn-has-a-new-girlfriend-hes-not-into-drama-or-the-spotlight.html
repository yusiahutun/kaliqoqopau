    Taylor Swift’s ex-boyfriend, Joe Alwyn, now has a new girlfriend as a source relays he’s “not into drama.”

    Since September 2023, Swifties have been head over heels for any glimpses of Taylor Swift together with her now-partner Travis Kelce. Despite the two going strong, fans had been anticipating the icon’s eleventh album, The Tortured Poets Department, to mostly be about her break-up with English actor Joe Alwyn, whom she dated for six years before their relationship ended in April 2023. Now that it’s here, however, lots of listeners have been quick to suggest there’s more material about others than him. Regardless, Joe has moved on too…

    Joe Alwyn is ‘dating a new girlfriend and happy’ after Taylor Swift break-up

    The 33-year-old actor of movies like The Favorite has been separated from Taylor for over a year now, and a source has confirmed to PEOPLE that “He’s dating and happy.”

    “Doing well” and “focused on work”, the source has also expressed that “He’s a great guy and not into drama in any way.”

    Since the release of The Tortured Poets Department, fans have speculated that songs such as ‘So Long, London’ are about him and the end of their long romance. Inevitably, Swifties have then been wondering if there are any hard feelings on his part.

    The source added Joe “certainly doesn’t talk poorly about [Taylor]… He was in love with her, and it just didn’t work out.” Back when they ended, a source told people it was due to “differences in their personalities.”

    It’s currently uncertain who the star is dating, but Joe does favor his privacy.

    “Joe loves acting,” the source highlighted, “but can’t stand the attention that comes with it. He’s not comfortable in the spotlight.”

    Although there’s no name just yet, fans have been quick to chime in with humor. “They say Joe’s dating,” one tweeted. “Wrong! He hasn’t hit me up yet and – if not me – then who?” they joked.

    With both Taylor and Joe dating other people, a certain long-running saga in the fandom may be close to an end.

    He has a new movie out this summer

    If you’re eager to see Joe doing his thing, you can when his upcoming movie Kinds Of Kindness reaches theaters on Friday, June 21st 2024.

    It’s an anthology movie telling a selection of different stories. Co-starring Emma Stone, Willem Dafoe, Jesse Plemons, Hunter Schafer, and more, it marks the latest film from Greek filmmaker Yorgos Lanthimos, who directed Joe in The Favorite and most recently worked with Emma on the fantasy-comedy Poor Things, which won her a second Oscar for Best Actress.

    It’s one of the most anticipated films of the summer, and Joe certainly plays a role in that excitement.
