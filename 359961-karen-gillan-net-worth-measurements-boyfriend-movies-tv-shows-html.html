    Karen Gillan biography

    Karen Sheila Gillan was born on November 28, 1987, in Inverness, Scotland, to Marie Paterson and Raymond Gillan. She decided to become an actress while still young, and when she was 16 years old, she joined Telford College in Edinburg.

    After two years, she moved to London where she studied at the Italia Conti Academy of Theatre Arts. During this time, she was scouted by a modelling agency and began her career as a model, but she was certain that her passion was in acting and not the former. As a model, she premiered at the London Fashion Week in 2007.
