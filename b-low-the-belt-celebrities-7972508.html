    I used to hate wearing belts when I was younger. As a growing child, it made sense to buy bigger pants so that you could get more wear out of them, but having to wear the belt to keep my pants up was just so uncomfortable. While I avoided belts through my teenage years, I have found myself hunting for quality belts as an adult. It’s no denying that belts are now trendier than ever, with celebs from Heidi Klum to Emily Ratajkowski making a statement with the leather accessory. My pursuit of the perfect belt has become even more crucial despite the answer being right in my face.

    As it turns out, there’s one under-the-radar belt brand that’s become a celebrity go-to over the years:  B-Low the Belt. Jennifer Lopez has been photographed wearing the brand’s double buckle Bri Bri Leather Belt over a long sleeve black jumpsuit, while Bella Hadid matched the coffee-colored Kennedy Leather Belt with her Adidas Sambas in France last year. But the A-list names don’t stop there: the stylish belts have also been spotted on Jessica Alba, Sofia Richie Grainge, and Jasmine Tookes.

    Now that I know the exact belt brand celebs have been donning, I took to B-Low The Belt's website and rounded up the eight styles I plan to add to my collection.

    B-Low The Belt Koda Mod Leather Belt

    I am a stan for a classic black belt with a unique buckle and keepers — it can take a T-shirt and trousers or jeans to the next level so effortlessly. That’s why it was love at first sight when I saw the Koda Mod Leather Belt. The smooth leather and the luxe gold hardware sold me immediately. 

    The belt comes in black, brown, and white with accenting gold hardware in sizes XS through XL. It has five adjustable holes for a custom fit and has a width of 1.5 inches, which is the standard width to fit most bottom belt loops.

    B-Low The Belt Angela Crystal Belt

    With all things sparkly and silver taking over thanks to Beyoncé’s Renaissance tour and the 2023 metallics trend, it only makes sense that the Angela Crystal Belt is on my list. You can give your denim some flair with the style, or take the Y2K route and wear it over a mini dress draped over the hips. It’s the perfect belt for those who aren’t afraid of a little attention and are looking to add a little razzle-dazzle to their ensemble. The belt is completely crystal-lined and fully adjustable at a 40-inch length. 

    B-Low The Belt Ophelia Gloss Leather Belt

    Looking to bring some shape back to your dresses or blazers? Add a belt, and more specifically, the two-in-one Ophelia Gloss Leather Belt. Belts aren’t just for trousers — they can take slouchy and oversized silhouettes and make them feel chic and tailored. It’s also a great way to breathe new life into the staple pieces you wear all the time because it adds a unique styling component and visual interest to any look. 

    The double strap belt comes in black and brown in sizes XS through XL. It has five adjustable holes on both straps and an asymmetrical double-ring buckle that’s finished in gold.
