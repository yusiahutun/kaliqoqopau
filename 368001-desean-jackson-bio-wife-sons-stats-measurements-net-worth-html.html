    Desean Jackson’s biography

    DeSean William Jackson, popularly known as DeSean Jackson, was born on December 1, 1986. He is the son of Bill and Gayle Jackson. He has an older brother, Byron, who is a former San Jose State wide receiver. DeSean’s father died of pancreatic cancer on May 14, 2009.

    Education

    DeSean went to the Long Beach Polytechnic High School, which is the same school his future Eagles teammate Winston Justice went to. While in high school, DeSean also ran track and played baseball.

    After high school, Desean had scholarship offers for California and the University of Southern California. He waited until the deadline and picked the University of California. While there, he was a prominent member of the California Golden Bears football team from 2005 to 2007.

    DeSean was expected to play for Cal's baseball team, but he decided to focus on football only due to the excellent start he had. He left California after the 2007 season and declared for the 2008 NFL Draft. To this day, he ranks third all-time at California for receiving yards with 2,423 and receiving touchdowns with 22.
