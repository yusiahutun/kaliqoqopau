    Selena Gomez is a dream in yellow oversized sweater and it's available to shop

    Selena Gomez is style goals right now, and the 30-year-old actress brought the sunshine to the 2023 iHeartRadio Music Awards, wearing a bright yellow sweater as she paid tribute via video to her bestie Taylor Swift.

    16 Jul 2024, 00:00

    The Only Murders in the Building star loves a Reformation buy

      Selena Gomez is style goals right now, and the 30-year-old actress brought the sunshine to the 2023 iHeartRadio Music Awards, wearing a bright yellow sweater as she paid tribute via video to her bestie Taylor Swift.

      The cashmere piece is from LA-based brand Reformation - a label you might recognise as it’s been seen on the likes of Jennifer Lopez, Hailey Bieber and Taylor herself.

      View post on Instagram 

      While Reformation is probably most famous for coveted summer dresses, the knitwear collection is not to be slept on. With bright hues like Selena’s, the pieces are suitable for any season.

      The Rare Beauty founder wore the Garrett turtleneck sweater in the shade Harvest. Also available in brown and grey, it features an oversized fit and is sustainably made from 90% recycled and 10% virgin cashmere. It retails for £318 or $268 and is still available in sizes XS - M, but it’s selling out fast.

      Garrett cashmere oversized turtleneck, £318/$268 at Reformation

      Clearly a fan of a Reformation knit, just a few months ago Selena was spotted in the brand’s Otto polo sweater while out in New York.

      Posing outside of Waverly Place, she captioned her Instagram post: 'Where it all began' delighting her OG fans who have been following her since her Disney days when she starred in .

      View post on Instagram 

      The Otto wool polo sweater is cut with a flattering collared neckline and side slits with rib details at the hem and cuffs. A preppier style than the Garrett, it costs £235 or $198 and is still available in most sizes. 

      Otto wool polo sweater,  £235/$198 at Reformation

      Selena paired hers with statement printed trousers but we'd also love it styled with straight-leg jeans or a spring-ready mini skirt.
