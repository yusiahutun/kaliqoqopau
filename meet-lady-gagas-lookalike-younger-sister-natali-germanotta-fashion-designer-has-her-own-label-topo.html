    Lady Gaga has reportedly cancelled her sister Natali Germanotta’s bachelorette party at famously outrageous New York nightclub The Box amid claims the venue sexually harassed its staff, according to US media.

    Court documents from 2022 show that a former staff member claimed managers “ordered and coerced female waitresses, performers, and employees to dress provocatively and travel throughout New York City in order to lure male customers to [The] Box, where they would be serviced with sexual acts, sexual intercourse, drugs, and excessive alcohol by other female employees who had been forced to perform these acts.”

    Although The Box has denied the allegations, the case is still ongoing.

    So we know Natali won’t be celebrating her upcoming nuptials with her girls at The Box, but here’s what we do know about Lady Gaga’s younger sis …

    Natali Germanotta’s background

    The Germanotta sisters were born to their parents Joe and Cynthia and raised in New York City’s Upper West Side, according to People magazine. The pair were reportedly enrolled in Sacred Heart, an all-girls Catholic school.

    Natali Germanotta is a fashion designer

    Gaga and her sister have a mutual love of fashion. According to People, Natali, 32, graduated from the Parsons School of Design in New York City in 2014 and created costumes for the off-Broadway show Simon Says during her degree. She’s also interned for designer Allison Parris, according to British Vogue, and worked as an assistant for her sister’s stylist, Brandon Maxwell. In 2021 she created her own fashion line, Topo Studio, per People, and her superstar sister has worn her designs on several occasions.

    She’s starred in her sister’s music videos

    You may recognise Germanotta from one of Lady Gaga’s music videos.

    Per People, Natali appeared in the “Telephone” music video Gaga did with Beyoncé in 2009, and also made a cameo in the video for “Joanne (Where Do You Think You’re Going?)” in 2018.
