    MAFS UK was filmed in London – take an exclusive look at where the cast stayed during season eight

      As the drama of Married at First Sight UK continues, you may be wondering where the eighth season has been filmed. The apartments the couples are staying in are located within Repton Gardens in Wembley Park, managed by Quintain Living.

      Hello! can now reveal an exclusive tour around the impressive building that houses an impressive 396 homes and provided the backdrop for the explosive show. Take a tour of the rooms...

      Quintain Living teamed up with John Lewis & Partners for sustainable furniture for the apartments, and the beautiful interiors can be admired throughout the season. 

      The MAFS apartment block

      Quintain Living teamed up with John Lewis & Partners for sustainable furniture for the apartments, and the beautiful interiors can be admired throughout the season.

      The MAFS apartment block lounges

      The carefully curated lounges are the perfect balance between cosy and modern with beautiful furniture like chic sideboards and contemporary L-shaped sofas.

      The MAFS apartment block kitchens

      The kitchens are sizeable with a natural oak laminate, with dark green cabinets in some apartments and a mix of green and grey in others.

      The MAFS apartment block bedrooms

      The contestants' bedrooms are bright and airy with stylish accessories like rattan bedside cabinets and cute lamps.

      The MAFS apartment block bathrooms

      It's all about clean and modern lines in the bathrooms with grey ceramic tiles keeping it pristine.

      The MAFS apartment block gardens

      As well as the individual homes, there are some rather impressive communal areas including a "central podium garden" with two playparks and hireable BBQ areas.

      The MAFS apartment club lounge

      There's also a 'Club Room' with a pool table, which is where we saw JJ and Ella's secret rendezvous on the show.

      The MAFS apartment block gym

      The gym on site is perfect for the cast to keep fit while they are taking part in the experiment – but we predict no squatting for Georges!

      Paul explained: "We are so hands on with the couples this year and go into their apartments to guide them along the way, which is a first."

      Speaking about the success of the show today, Paul said: "I had no idea the show would be so huge when we first started.
