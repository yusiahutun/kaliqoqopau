From buzz cuts to curls, the list of men's haircuts for square faces is extensive.

    The square shape is one of the most common face shapes for men. Its sharp, angular jawline characterises it, with defined cheekbones and a broad forehead that provide it with a masculine appearance. In addition, the proportions are nearly equal in length.

    11 best men's haircuts for square faces

    The square face shape is considered to be the most masculine face shape. Its characteristics of sharp and bonny edges provide a challenging task when choosing the ideal haircut. Experts recommend selecting hairstyles that soften the look.

    Here are the 11 best hairstyles for a square-face male.

    1. The comb over

    If you are looking for men's haircuts for square faces with medium-length hair, the comb-over is the ideal choice. The comb-over is a famous men's hairstyle that features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides. The hair on the top is typically medium-length and straight.

    The comb-over can comprise any colour, but it's often seen in black, brown or blonde. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle and perfectly suits the male square face shape.

    2. The side swept with part

    The side swept with part hairdo features a straight, longer top with trimmed shorter sides. The hair on the top is long, providing contrast to the thin, shaved sides. The longer hair on top is swept to one side.

    The side swept can be any colour and requires regular maintenance on the sides to keep it short. If you are looking for men's haircuts for square faces with straight hair, the side swept with part is an ideal choice.

    3. Slicked back tapered

    The slicked-back tapered is one of the most stylish haircuts for square-face-shaped men. It features thick, straight, long-top hair with an undercut. The slicked-back pointed features best with dark hair as it showcases the patterns better.

    The hair is slicked back and features highlights at the top. Such a design requires regular maintenance to keep the top part gelled and in place, while the sides require constant tapering.

    4. Back and side swept

    The back and side swept men's hairstyle is among the most common designs. It features wavy long hair. The best colour combination for this design is dark brown. The hair is styled in a slicked-back manner while keeping the hair volume visible.

    The back and side-swept men's hairstyle requires minimal maintenance because no shaving is involved. This design is ideal for square-faced shapes.

    5. The top-knot bun

    The top-knot bun is a versatile haircut that is a breed between an undercut and a man bun. It features long, wavy hair. The colour of this hairstyle is dark brown, styled into a bun by pulling the hair to the top and tying it with a band.

    Read also

    The top-knot bun is easy to maintain as it requires no trimming, no professional styling and no unique products.

    6. Modern pompadour

    The modern pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that features the classic variation mixed with a modern design like the undercut. The design features longer wavy hair on the top and back. The hair is styled in a slicked-back fashion, but the hair is left loose to achieve volume.

    The maintenance for this hairstyle is high as it requires regular shaving and professional styling for the top hair.

    7. Comma hairstyle

    The comma hairstyle is a popular design from Korea. It features long and wavy hair that looks great on black-coloured hair. The styling involves using fringes that are swept to the sides and curled to resemble the shape of a comma.

    The comma hairstyle requires regular maintenance to keep the fringes the required length. If you are looking for a Korean hairstyle for a square-face male, the comma hairdo is ideal.

    8. Short fade undercut

    The short fade undercut is one of the best square face shape hairstyles for males without a beard. The design features wavy medium-length hair that you can style in different colours. The short fade undercut features textured bangs that complement the face shape. The hairstyle requires regular maintenance to maintain the desired design.

    9. Short, messy wavy haircut

    The short, messy, wavy haircut offers a texture that regular designs can't. The design features curly medium-length hair that provides volume, movement and natural flow. The hair is styled in a messy, tousled fashion that creates bangs.

    This ragged design requires minimal maintenance as no shaving is required, only styling using various hair products.

    10. Faux hawk

    The faux hawk is one of the most popular hairstyles around. The design features short sides with longer tops, with the hair texture varying from straight to wavy. The faux hawk is versatile and can be worn in any colour. The design features styling the hair like a Mohawk but without the longer back.

    Read also

    The faux hawk requires regular maintenance to keep the top intact, and the sides tapered. This design is ideal for men with square face shapes and can complement a clean-shaven look well.

    11. Buzz cut

    The buzz cut is one of the most popular haircuts of all time. Loved for its simplicity, the buzz cut features concise hair that can suit any hair texture. You can wear the buzz cut with your natural hair colour or go bold with a dye of your choice.

    The buzz cut is easy to achieve; request your barber to set the shaving machine to a number one or number two setting and let them shave your hair evenly all through. This design is among the easiest and budget-friendly haircuts as it requires minimal maintenance.

    Wrapping up

    Men's haircuts for square faces comprise of different hairdos that suit well the strong jawline and broad forehead the face shape characterises. They include designs like the classic crew cut, the buzz cut, the faux hawk, the modern pompadour and more.
