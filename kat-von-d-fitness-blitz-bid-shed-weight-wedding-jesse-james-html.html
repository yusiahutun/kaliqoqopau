    After only just announcing their engagement last week, Jesse James and Kat Von D are allegedly planning a February wedding.

    And Kat  is wasting no time in starting preparations for her nuptials to fiancé Jesse James.

    The curvy tattoo artist has been hitting trendy Equinox gym in West Hollywood on a daily basis in an effort to slim down.

    Slimming down: Kat Von D is trying to lose weight before her wedding next month

    A trainer at the gym spoke exclusively to the MailOnline today and revealed that Kat is determined to be in the best shape of her life for the ceremony.

    'She's trying really hard to get ready for her wedding,' confirms the source.

    The couple are apparently planning a 'small wedding'  next month somewhere in California.

    'Jesse and Kat don't see any reason why they should wait to trade vows,' a source close to the couple told Radar Online earlier this week.

    Curvy: Kat Von D is hitting trendy Equinox gym on a daily basis to get in shape

    'They are madly in love with each other and want to make everything official.'

    The couple, who have been dating for just over four months, announced their engagement yesterday with Jesse telling People that 2010 was the 'best year of my  life.'

    That came as a surprise to many as 2010 saw the West Coast Chopper boss split from wife Sandra Bullock, weeks after she won an Academy Award in March for her role in The Blind Side.

    Their split followed reports of the 41-year-old's extra marital affairs and their divorce became final in June.

    Diamonds are forever: Kat flashes her new engagement ring and blue hair style

    Jesse is believed to have slept with at least six other women while being married to the actress and was vilified in the press for his actions, even losing his reality show West Coast Choppers.

    Engaged: Kat shows off her engagement ring with Jesse last week

    But he said that far from being a terrible year it had been 'the best'.

    'You know sometimes the public and press gets it wrong,' he told People magazine. 'This is one of those times.

    '2010 was actually the best year of my life because I fell in love with my best friend. An amazing woman who stood behind me when the world turned their backs.

    'I have never met anyone so kind and loving and committed to making the world a better place every day.

    'My love for her is beyond description. So honoured that she said "yes." Growing old with her is going to be a f****** blast!'

    The couple have made no secret of their love over their respective Twitter accounts, with Jeese yesterday posting a black and white picture of his new love, accompanied by a gushing caption.

    'Epic day comes to an end. Closing my eyes to dream about @thekatvon.' 

    Kat has been equally open, yesterday tweeting:

    'Waking up to what @FreeJesseJames said about our engagement this morning brought tears to my eyes, in the best way possible,' she wrote.

    'I guess the 'kat' is outta the bag eh?'

    Jesse responded: 'I only said it cuz it's true!'

    In love: Jesse James yesterday tweeted a pictured of his new fiancee, accompanied by the caption: 'Closing my eyes to dream about @thekatvond'

    However, Kat appears undecided about whether she will follow Jesse to Austin, Texas, where he recently moved.

    'If only it were that easy,' she said. 'Having two places we call home will be good for now. In the end, home is where the heart is, and my heart's with him.'

    Meanwhile, Jesse's ex mistress Michelle 'Bombshell' McGee did have a warning for Kat.

    'Kat what in the world are you thinking?' she told Radar. 'I was with Jesse during his marriage - the guy's not faithful, he doesn't have a faithful bone in his body!

    'f he did it to me, if he did it to Sandra, he's gonna do it to you, too - once a cheater always a cheater. He cheated on his wife, numerous times.'
