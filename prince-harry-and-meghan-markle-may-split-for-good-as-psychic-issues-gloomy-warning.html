    Prince Harry and Meghan Markle may 'split for good' as psychic issues gloomy warning

    Exclusive: Celebrity Tidbit sat down with a psychic to predict the future of arguably one of the most controversial couples of 2023, and its not good news, as he predicts a split between Prince Harry and Meghan Markle.

    20 May 2024, 00:00

    Exclusive: Celebrity Tidbit sat down with a psychic to predict the future of arguably one of the most controversial couples of 2023, and it’s not good news, as he predicts a split between Prince Harry and Meghan Markle.

    The couple met in the Summer of 2016, and it was love at first sight. Two years later, they tied the knot in an extravagant royal wedding but ended up leaving the institution and moved to LA. Since then, Prince Harry hasn’t had the best relationship with his family members. Although he and Meghan Markle have always stayed strong. However, celebrity psychic Adam Norton has predicted it may all come to an end this year.

    Harry and Meghan will ‘split’ this year – celebrity psychic predicts

    Unfortunately, for Harry and Meghan stans, Adam predicts bad news in the year ahead for their relationship, and he says Meghan will ‘go back to work’.

    “Definitely seeing a separation ahead for Harry and Meghan, and I actually feel Meghan will return back to work, so her career is about to take off in a different direction,” he told Celebrity Tidbit.

    He says we can expect the former Suits actress to venture behind the camera too: “I see some movie/acting work ahead for her, so definitely expect to see her on the big screen. Both behind and in front of the camera, so there’s some acting work she’ll be doing, but also a little bit of directing / behind the scene action.”

    The couple moved over to Los Angeles shortly after leaving the royal family. Experts have since been saying Meghan wanted to live the Hollywood lifestyle.

    Since leaving, she’s visited the UK once, during the death of Queen Elizabeth II. Harry, on the other hand, has visited a few more times, including for his father King Charles‘ coronation last year. Norton also predicts the Duke will be missing his home country, and may ‘regret’ how things went down.

    This year, Prince Harry released his tell-all memoir Spare, which is said to have further jeopardized his relationship with both his father, King Charles, and his brother, Prince William.

    However, celebrity psychic Adam predicts he’ll start to regret his move overseas, and how things turned out with his family in the year to come.

    Divorce rumors have been swirling since last year

    It comes after divorce rumors surrounded the pair last fall, following Meghan Markle being caught without wearing her engagement ring.

    Fans were left concerned it meant something was happening behind the scenes.

    However, the real reason she didn’t wear her ring was actually quite boring.

    Then, Vanity Fair’s former editor came out to claim he believes they would split as well. He blasted the couple, alleging he thinks their marriage would last years and not decades.

    According to insiders, this deeply hurt Prince Harry. The duke was apparently concerned due to the former editor’s previous job, his comments would be seen as having truth in them when it was simply opinion.
