    For 37, she's certainly not in bad shape.

    But it was a rather fuller-figured Neve Campbell who emerged from the Hawaii sea compared to the toned and taught physique she displayed in her 1998 film Wild Things.

    Clad in a string black bikini, the actress exhibited her curvier frame as she relaxed on the beach before towelling off following her dip in the ocean.

    Neave, who is reprising her role as Sidney Prescott in Scream 4, caused a stir in her lesbian pool scene with Denise Richards in Wild Things.

    These days the Canadian star bases herself in London and is married to British actor John Light, who she married in 2007.

    The actress - who has a Scottish father and a Dutch mother - is having a break as she's just finished shooting the fourth installment in the Scream horror franchise, the clever scary movies that sparked a string of imitations.

    It also features Hayden Panettiere, Rory Culkin, Emma Roberts, as well as Courteney Cox and estranged husband David Arquette.

    You can see the movie's trailer, which has just been released, by clicking on the link at the bottom of the story.

    It opens in cinemas across the UK on 15 April.

    Neve's next project meanwhile is a biopic of Four Seasons composer Antonio Vivaldi, alongside, Gerard Depardieu, Alfred Molina, Jacqueline Bisset, Elle Fanning and Carice Van Houten. 

    It tells the story of when the composer, then a priest, became the music teacher at a school for the illegitimate daughters of courtesans in 17th Century Venice. 

    It's scheduled for release next year.
