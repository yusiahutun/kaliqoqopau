    Starla Baskett’s Biography, Nationality and Religion

    Starla Baskett is an American entrepreneur and celebrity mother to the famous Hollywood actor Zac Efron. She was born on January 14, 1949, in the United States, where she was also raised by her parents, Robert Harold Baskett and June Helene Glidden. She has two sisters, Maisie and Tana Baskett, with whom she grew up and with whom she maintains contact.

    Regarding her ethnicity and religion, Starla belongs to a Caucasian ethnic group, was raised in a Jewish family, and follows Judaism as a religion.

    Starla Baskett’s Educational Background and Career

    The celebrity mom attended high school and is a college graduate with a Bachelor of Arts degree. After getting her degree, she worked as a secretary at a nuclear power facility in Diablo Canyon. 

    She has now retired from the official workspace and has focused more on entrepreneurship and her children. Her business’s exact details are unknown, but she has been successful, with a reported net worth of $5 million. 

    Who Is Starla Baskett’s Husband?

    Starla Baskett was married to Zac’s father, David Efron, for a very long time, and the couple lived in Arroyo Grande, California. She met David at the nuclear power plant, where he worked as an engineer. They got married and stayed together for over three decades before getting a divorce in 2015, citing “irreconcilable differences.”

    After her divorce from David, Starla did not marry or date any other man. She remains single and maintains a low profile, making it difficult to ascertain if she has had any affairs or relationships. On the other hand, David remarried and started a new family some years after their divorce.
