    With a fibreglass exterior complemented by chrome-plated details, and weighing a hefty 1,460kg, the Bun Van, handcrafted and hand-tailored in Portugal, is as sturdy as it is aesthetically pleasing. The exterior is made from a resin mould and the Circu team are more than willing to offer customisation. A bigger van that accommodates a king-size bed or bunk bed for your child’s playmates is also possible. (In its standard dimensions, the Bun Van measures 4 metres wide, 1.85 metres deep and more than two metres tall.)

    Using a custom mould to make your toddler’s bespoke Bun Van can lift the asking price from its base level around US$39,000, to beyond six figures.

    Glossy palisander wood veneer sets the tone for the interiors. Palisander has a deep rosewood hue that gives that sense of cosiness necessary for any childhood hideout. Besides the bed, there’s also space for a TV, desk, sofa, a few storage compartments and a mini fridge for drinks and midnight snacks.
