    Just for you

    You customize your diet, your music playlist and your Netflix queue, but you’ve never personalized your shampoo and conditioner—until now. Jodi Dery, owner of South Florida’s Cloud 10 salons, co-created ProfilePRO, a customizable hair care line available on . The site prompts a questionnaire asking about hair types, textures, lengths and preferred scents, so bottles match all of your hair’s needs. Cloud 10 salons in Delray and Boca Raton also offer the products in-store under the Heavenly Hair Care line.



    Color secret

    Senior stylist Destiny De La Paz of McAllister Spa offers the scoop on Olaplex, a formula she’s been using since June that’s revolutionizing the hair coloring industry.

    Q: Why Olaplex?

    A: Where the coloring process previously broke down bonds in the hair, the Olaplex treatment is now able to swoop in, acting as a bond multiplier to rebuild those bonds, preventing damage and even leaving hair stronger and healthier than it would be otherwise. Stylists are now able to take clients lighter than they ever could have dreamed of with extremely minimal damage.

    Q: What’s your favorite look you’ve created with the formula?

    A: One of my favorite services to do with Olaplex is a damage-free, double-process blonde with a pastel overlay.

    Q: Are there concerns when using it?

    A: Olaplex can slightly alter the level of color applied to the hair, so it is critical to visit an experienced stylist with specific knowledge on how to formulate with it.
