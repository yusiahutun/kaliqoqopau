    Janice Dickinson was born on February 15, 1955.

    Martha Janice Dickinson, commonly known as Janice Dickinson, was born on February 15, 1955, in Brooklyn, New York City.

    She is often referred to as the “world’s first supermodel.”

    She was a judge on the reality TV show, America’s Next Top Model.

    Janice Dickinson became well-known to a new generation of viewers as a judge on the hit reality TV show, America’s Next Top Model, from 2003 to 2006.

    Janice Dickinson has authored several books, including a memoir.
