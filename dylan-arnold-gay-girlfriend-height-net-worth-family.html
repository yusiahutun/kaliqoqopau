    Dylan Arnold On You

    In 2020, Dylan Arnold was announced as a new cast member for You citing he would be joining in a regular capacity as the character of Theo.

    Yes, Dylan plays Theo Engler, a troubled college student with an undeniable crush on  Victoria Pedretti’s character Love Quinn and a strained and complicated relationship with his stepfather.

    Dylan’s character makes appearances throughout the 10-episode season as he becomes entangled in Joe Goldberg (played by Penn Badgley) and Love Quinn’s toxic relationship.

    As Deadline puts it, Theo is wise and perceptive, but vulnerable and troubled, with a proneness to get tangled up in his loved ones’ problems and to drown his own in controlled substances and dangerous behavior.
