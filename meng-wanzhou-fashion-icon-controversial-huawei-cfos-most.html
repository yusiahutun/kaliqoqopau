    Meng Wanzhou, CFO of Chinese tech giant Huawei, was recently freed from Canada after a deal with the US Department of Justice. The tech executive, who was previously under house arrest in her multimillion-dollar mansion in Vancouver, travelled to Shenzhen on a chartered Air China flight on September 25. Two Canadians detained in China were also released and returned to their home country.

    The best and worst dressed celebrities at the Met Gala 2021

    During her three-year detention in Canada, the daughter of the tech company’s founder, Ren Zhengfei, attended numerous court hearings. And one thing we noticed over the years was her incredible style transformation, going from simple hoodies and trainers to well-curated high fashion ensembles. Her refined looks featured clothing and accessories from luxury labels Hermès, Chanel, Fendi, Miu Miu as well as sky-high stilettos from Jimmy Choo, Christian Louboutin and Roger Vivier – together with her ankle monitor.

    And although Meng went through an ordeal weightier than her appearance might belie, the internet is still fascinated with the powerful attitude the top executive exuded through her fashion choices. Chinese netizens interpreted Meng’s outfits as her way of showing her resilience and even silently protesting.

    Here are some of the most memorable pieces Meng wore during her 1,028-day detention …

    One of the most memorable moments of the saga was when Meng touched down in the Chinese city of Shenzhen, home to Huawei’s headquarters. She stepped out of the plane in a scarlet red dress by American designer Carolina Herrera. Chinese citizens praised the tech executive for wearing red – a hue that’s a nod to the Chinese flag and culture.

    “Where there is a Chinese flag, there is a beacon of faith,” Meng said in a speech at the airport where a crowd welcomed her homecoming. “If faith has a colour, it must be China red.”

    Meng’s other two statement-making accessories were her red Chinese flag brooch and the new black Huawei Watch 3.

    During an appearance at the Supreme Court of British Columbia in Vancouver in April, Meng showed up in a bold pink tweed suit by Chanel, fresh from the French brand’s pre-spring 2021 collection.

    She was also spotted in another classic tweed jacket in vibrant yellow from French maison Chanel’s resort 2021 collection.

    In the same month, the 49-year-old wore a black and white coat dress featuring a youthful peter pan collar from Miu Miu’s pre-autumn 2020 collection.

    In December last year, the Huawei executive sported a burgundy coat from Italian brand Max Mara and a golden leaf motif brooch from French maison Goossens.

    We dug this an all-white businessy ensemble from Ralph & Russo.

    The ankle bracelet is not the only eye-catching item here. Meng’s dazzling shoe collection – including pieces from some of the world’s best shoemakers Jimmy Choo, Roger Vivier, Manolo Blahnik and Christian Louboutin – are equally headline-making.

    Meng opted for a Fendi shawl to stay cosy and warm while dealing with the Canadian weather.

    For her court attendance in October last year, Meng was wrapped in a colourful scarf from French maison Hermès.

    Meng also carried an Hermès Lindy handbag in beige for another court appearance.

    In May, Meng wore an elegant black and gold-accented boat neck dress from Italian house Gucci.
